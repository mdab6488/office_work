## 8. E-commerce SEO

E-commerce SEO is the practice of optimizing online stores to rank higher in search engine results, driving organic traffic and increasing sales. Unlike regular SEO, e-commerce SEO deals with unique challenges like duplicate content, large product catalogs, and complex site structures.

### Why E-commerce SEO Matters
- **70% of online experiences** begin with a search engine
- **Google Shopping** drives significant e-commerce traffic
- **Organic search** provides long-term, cost-effective traffic
- Better **user experience** = higher conversions
