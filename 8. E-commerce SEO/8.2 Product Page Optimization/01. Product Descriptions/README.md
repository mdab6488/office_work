#### Product Description Best Practices

**Length Guidelines:**
- **Electronics/Tech:** 300-500 words
- **Apparel:** 150-300 words  
- **High-ticket items** (furniture, appliances): 500-1000 words
- **Unique/niche products:** 300-700 words

**Keyword Optimization:**
```
Primary keyword: "men's waterproof hiking boots"
Secondary keywords: 
- "waterproof hiking boots for men"
- "men's backpacking boots"
- "waterproof trail boots"
- "insulated hiking boots"

Natural Integration:
"These men's waterproof hiking boots are built for serious trails. 
The Gore-Tex membrane keeps your feet dry in any weather, while the 
Vibram sole provides superior traction on rocky terrain. Perfect for 
backpacking trips or day hikes, these waterproof trail boots feature 
200g of insulation for cold-weather comfort."
```

**Content Structure:**
1. Opening hook (emotional appeal)
2. Key benefits (what problem it solves)
3. Features (how it works)
4. Use cases (who it's for)
5. Specifications (technical details)
6. Call to action
