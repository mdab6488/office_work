
### Product Reviews

**What Are Product Reviews (for Link Building)?**

Getting your products reviewed by bloggers, journalists, and industry publications to earn backlinks and exposure.

---

#### Real-Life Examples

**Example 1: Anker's Review Strategy**

**Approach:**
- Sends products to tech reviewers on YouTube
- Provides review units to major tech blogs
- Gives exclusive early access to new products
- Creates dedicated PR relationships

**Results:**
- Reviews on TechCrunch, The Verge, Wirecutter
- Links from Domain Authority 80+ sites
- Product mentions generate 15,000+ monthly searches
- Reviews drive 25% of product discovery

**Review Sites They Target:**
- Tom's Guide (DA 85)
- PCMag (DA 89)
- TechRadar (DA 84)
- Individual YouTube tech channels (500K+ subs)

#### How to Get Product Reviews and Links

**Step 1: Identify Review Opportunities**

**Types of Reviewers:**
1. **Industry Publications** (High DA, Hard to Get)
   - Major magazines and websites
   - Example: Wirecutter, Consumer Reports

2. **Niche Blogs** (Medium DA, Moderate Difficulty)
   - Blogs focused on your industry
   - Example: Running blog for running shoes

3. **YouTube Reviewers** (Variable DA, Moderate Difficulty)
   - Tech reviewers, unboxers, lifestyle channels
   - Example: MKBHD, Unbox Therapy

4. **Micro-Influencers** (Low DA, Easy)
   - 10K-100K followers
   - Higher engagement rates
   - More likely to review

**Step 2: Find Reviewers**

**Search Operators:**
```
"[product category] review" blog
"best [product category]" inurl:blog
"[product category] reviews" site:.org
intitle:"review" [product category]
```

**Tools to Use:**
- Ahrefs Content Explorer
- BuzzSumo
- Social media hashtag search
- YouTube search
- Amazon reviewer lists

**Step 3: Outreach Email Template**

```
Subject: [Product Name] Review Unit - [Your Company]

Hi [Name],

I'm [Your Name] from [Company]. I've been following your work on [specific content] and really appreciated your review of [competitor product].

We've just launched [Product Name], which [unique value proposition]. I think it would resonate with your audience because [reason].

I'd love to send you a complimentary unit to review. No strings attached - your honest opinion is what matters.

If you're interested, I can ship it to you this week.

Some details about [Product]:
- [Key Feature 1]
- [Key Feature 2]
- [Retail Price]

Let me know if this interests you!

Best,
[Your Name]
[Contact Info]
```

**Step 4: Product Seeding Campaign**

**Budget Approach:**
- Small Budget (<$1,000): 20 micro-influencers
- Medium Budget ($1,000-$5,000): 50 micro + 10 mid-tier
- Large Budget (>$5,000): 100+ influencers + major publications

**Expected Results:**
- 30% will respond
- 60% of those will publish reviews
- 40% of reviews will include links
- Final link rate: ~7-10% of outreach

**Example ROI:**
- Send 100 products at $30 each = $3,000
- Get 10 links from DA 40+ sites
- Cost per link: $300 (very good for quality links)

**Step 5: Make It Easy for Reviewers**

**Provide:**
- High-resolution product images
- Product specifications
- Key talking points (not scripted)
- Company background
- Press page URL
- Contact for questions

**Create a Press Kit:**
```
/press/
  - company-info.html
  - product-images.html
  - founder-bio.html
  - press-mentions.html
  - contact.html
```
