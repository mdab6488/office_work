# E-commerce SEO: Content Strategy & Link Building

## 8.6 E-commerce Link Building

Link building is crucial for e-commerce SEO but more challenging than for content sites. Your product pages won't naturally attract links, so you need strategic approaches.

## Bringing It All Together: Content + Link Building Strategy

**Integrated 12-Month E-commerce SEO Plan**

### Months 1-3: Foundation
**Content:**
- Create 5 cornerstone buying guides
- Launch 3 comparison pages (top products)
- Publish 8 blog posts (2 per week)

**Link Building:**
- Set up affiliate program
- Send products to 20 reviewers
- Partner with 10 micro-influencers

**Expected Results:**
- 30-50 backlinks
- 2,000 monthly organic visits

---

### Months 4-6: Expansion
**Content:**
- Add 10 more comparison pages
- Create 3 comprehensive guides
- Publish 12 blog posts
- Update existing content

**Link Building:**
- Recruit 50 affiliates
- Send products to 30 more reviewers
- Partner with 20 additional influencers
- Start ambassador program (top 5)

**Expected Results:**
- 100-150 total backlinks
- 5,000 monthly organic visits

---

### Months 7-9: Scaling
**Content:**
- Expand blog to 3 posts per week
- Create video content from blog posts
- Build 15 more comparison pages
- Launch resource hub

**Link Building:**
- Scale to 100 affiliates
- Major publication outreach
- 50 new influencer partnerships
- Guest posting campaign

**Expected Results:**
- 300-400 total backlinks
- 12,000 monthly organic visits

---

### Months 10-12: Optimization
**Content:**
- Update all buying guides
- Refresh comparison pages with new data
- Create ultimate guides (10,000+ words)
- Launch YouTube channel

**Link Building:**
- 200+ active affiliates
- Maintain top influencer relationships
- Broken link building
- Digital PR campaign

**Expected Results:**
- 600-800 total backlinks
- 25,000+ monthly organic visits
- Domain Authority increase of 15-20 points

---

## Key Takeaways

### E-commerce Content:
1. **Buying guides capture top-of-funnel traffic** and establish expertise
2. **Comparison pages target high-intent users** ready to buy
3. **Blog content builds authority** and creates linkable assets
4. **Quality over quantity** - one great guide beats 10 mediocre posts

### E-commerce Link Building:
1. **Product reviews generate targeted backlinks** from relevant sites
2. **Influencer partnerships create ongoing link opportunities** and brand awareness
3. **Affiliate programs scale link building** while driving sales
4. **Combine strategies for best results** - reviews + influencers + affiliates

### Success Factors:
- **Patience**: SEO takes 6-12 months to show significant results
- **Consistency**: Regular content + outreach beats sporadic efforts
- **Quality**: 100 great links beat 1,000 spammy links
- **Relationships**: Long-term partnerships > one-off transactions
- **Measurement**: Track everything, optimize continuously
