
### AnswerThePublic

**What It Is:** Free/Paid ($99/month Pro) visual keyword research tool showing question-based queries.

**Best For:**
- Finding content ideas
- Understanding what people ask about your topic
- Creating FAQ sections
- Voice search optimization

**How It Works:**
- Shows questions (who, what, where, when, why, how)
- Displays prepositions (for, with, to, etc.)
- Shows comparisons (vs, versus, or, and)
- Alphabetical suggestions

#### Real-Life Example: Content Marketing Agency

**Scenario:** You need 50 blog topics for a client in the fitness industry.

**Using AnswerThePublic:**

1. **Enter Seed Keyword:** "home workout"

2. **Questions Output:**
   ```
   How:
   - how to do home workouts effectively
   - how to stay motivated for home workouts
   - how to create a home workout routine
   
   What:
   - what equipment for home workouts
   - what are the best home workouts for weight loss
   - what home workouts burn the most calories
   
   Why:
   - why home workouts are better
   - why home workouts don't work
   - why am I not seeing results from home workouts
   
   Can:
   - can home workouts build muscle
   - can home workouts replace gym
   - can home workouts get you ripped
   ```

3. **Create Content Strategy:**
   ```
   Article 1: "How to Create the Perfect Home Workout Routine (Beginner's Guide)"
   - Answers: "how to create a home workout routine"
   - Intent: Informational
   - Estimated volume: 600 searches/month
   
   Article 2: "Home Workouts vs Gym: Which One Actually Works Better?"
   - Answers: "can home workouts replace gym", "why home workouts are better"
   - Intent: Commercial investigation
   - Estimated volume: 1,200 searches/month combined
   
   Article 3: "Why Your Home Workouts Aren't Working (5 Common Mistakes)"
   - Answers: "why home workouts don't work", "why am I not seeing results"
   - Intent: Informational (problem-solving)
   - Estimated volume: 400 searches/month
   ```

4. **Combine with Google Search Volume:**
   ```
   Take AnswerThePublic questions → Put into Google Keyword Planner → Get volume data
   
   Now you have:
   - 50 content ideas (from AnswerThePublic)
   - Search volume for each (from Keyword Planner)
   - Priority list based on volume + relevance
   ```

**Real Result:**
- Created 50 blog posts over 6 months
- All based on real questions people ask
- Content matches search intent perfectly
- 30 articles ranking page 1 within 8 months
- Client traffic: 5,000 → 35,000 monthly visitors
