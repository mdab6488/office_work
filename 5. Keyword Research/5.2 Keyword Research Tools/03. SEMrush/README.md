
### SEMrush

**What It Is:** All-in-one marketing toolkit ($119.95-$449.95/month) with strong PPC and competitor features.

**Best For:**
- Competitive intelligence
- Tracking keyword rankings
- PPC research
- International SEO

**Unique Features:**
- Advertising research (see competitor's Google Ads)
- Position tracking with daily updates
- Brand monitoring
- Traffic analytics

#### Real-Life Example: Local Business Competition

**Scenario:** You own a plumbing company in Denver competing with 5 established competitors.

**Using SEMrush:**

1. **Competitor Keyword Analysis:**
   ```
   Enter: competitorplumbing.com
   
   SEMrush reveals:
   - They rank for 847 keywords
   - Top keyword: "emergency plumber Denver" (1,900 searches/month)
   - They're position 3
   - Their page: Basic service page, 400 words
   
   Your opportunity:
   - Create ultimate guide: "Emergency Plumbing in Denver: 24/7 Service Guide"
   - Include: pricing transparency, response times, customer reviews
   - Add: Video showing your team, emergency checklist download
   - Result: Can outrank them with better content
   ```

2. **Finding Their Paid Keywords:**
   ```
   Advertising Research:
   
   Competitor spends $8,000/month on Google Ads for:
   - "water heater repair Denver" (CPC: $25.00)
   - "drain cleaning service" (CPC: $18.50)
   - "pipe burst emergency" (CPC: $32.00)
   
   Your strategy:
   Instead of competing in expensive PPC, rank organically:
   - Target same keywords with SEO
   - Create comprehensive service pages
   - Save $96,000/year in ad spend
   - Get same customers through organic ranking
   ```

3. **Position Tracking:**
   ```
   Track 30 of your most important keywords:
   
   Daily updates show:
   Week 1: "Denver plumber" = Position 15
   Week 4: "Denver plumber" = Position 11
   Week 8: "Denver plumber" = Position 7
   Week 12: "Denver plumber" = Position 4
   
   You: See progress, stay motivated, adjust strategy
   ```

**Real Business Impact:**
- Stopped spending $8,000/month on ads
- Invested $3,000/month in SEO instead
- Net savings: $5,000/month
- After 8 months: Ranking page 1 for 23 high-value keywords
- Organic leads: 40/month (previously 0)
