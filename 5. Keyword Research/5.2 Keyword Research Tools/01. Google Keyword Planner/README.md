
### Google Keyword Planner

**What It Is:** Free tool from Google Ads for keyword ideas and search volume data.

**Best For:**
- Beginners with $0 budget
- Google Ads campaign planning
- Getting official Google search volume data

**Limitations:**
- Requires Google Ads account (free to create)
- Shows volume ranges instead of exact numbers (unless you spend money on ads)
- Limited competitive analysis
- No keyword difficulty score

#### Real-Life Tutorial: Finding Keywords for a Bakery

**Scenario:** You own "Sweet Dreams Bakery" in Austin, Texas, and want local customers.

**Step-by-Step:**

1. **Setup:**
   - Go to ads.google.com
   - Click "Tools" → "Keyword Planner"
   - Choose "Discover new keywords"

2. **Input Your Ideas:**
   ```
   Seed keywords you enter:
   - custom birthday cakes Austin
   - wedding cakes near me
   - gluten free bakery
   - cupcakes delivery
   ```

3. **What Google Shows You:**
   ```
   Keyword: "custom birthday cakes Austin"
   Avg. monthly searches: 100-1K
   Competition: Low
   CPC: $2.10
   
   Related keywords Google suggests:
   - "birthday cake delivery Austin" (100-1K searches)
   - "best birthday cakes Austin" (100-1K searches)
   - "cake shops in Austin TX" (1K-10K searches)
   - "custom cakes near me" (10K-100K searches)
   ```

4. **Filter Strategy:**
   ```
   Apply filters:
   ✅ Location: Austin, Texas (25-mile radius)
   ✅ Language: English
   ✅ Search networks: Google only
   ✅ Competition: Low to Medium
   ```

5. **Export Your List:**
   - Download 50 relevant keywords
   - Sort by "Low competition" first
   - Prioritize local keywords with buying intent

**Result:** You now have data showing "custom birthday cakes Austin" gets 500 searches/month with low competition = perfect target.
