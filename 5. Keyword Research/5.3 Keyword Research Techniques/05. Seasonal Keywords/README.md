
### Seasonal Keywords

**What It Is:**
Keywords with search volume that spikes during specific times of the year, holidays, seasons, or events.

**Why They Matter:**
- Predictable traffic patterns
- Less year-round competition
- High intent during peak season
- Opportunity for timely content
- Can dominate during peak periods

**Types of Seasonal Keywords:**

1. **Holiday-Based**
   - Christmas, Valentine's Day, Halloween, etc.

2. **Season-Based**
   - Summer, Winter, Spring, Fall activities

3. **Event-Based**
   - Tax season, Back to school, Wedding season

4. **Industry-Specific Cycles**
   - "file taxes" (January-April)
   - "pool maintenance" (April-September)
   - "furnace repair" (October-February)

**Real-Life Example:**

**Scenario:** You run an e-commerce store selling costumes

**Seasonal Strategy:**

```
Keyword: "halloween costumes for kids"
Peak Month: September-October
Off-Season: November-August

Strategy Timeline:

June-July (Preparation Phase):
- Create content: "Top Halloween Costume Trends 2025"
- Build category pages
- Gather product inventory
- Start building backlinks

August (Early Push):
- Publish: "DIY Halloween Costume Ideas"
- Target: "early bird halloween costumes"
- Launch email campaigns

September (Peak Ramp-Up):
- Target: "halloween costumes for kids"
- Push: "last minute halloween costume delivery"
- Paid ads: Increase budget

October (Peak Season):
- Target: "halloween costumes near me"
- "same day halloween costume delivery"
- Maximum ad spend

November (Post-Season):
- Target: "clearance halloween costumes"
- Collect customer reviews
- Plan improvements for next year
```

**Pro Tips for Seasonal Keywords:**

1. **Publish Content 2-3 Months Early**
   - Google needs time to index and rank
   - Build authority before peak season

2. **Use Google Trends**
   - Identify exact spike timing
   - Compare year-over-year patterns
   - Find related rising queries

3. **Evergreen Content with Seasonal Updates**
   - "Best Christmas Gift Ideas [2025]"
   - Update year, keep core content
   - Maintains historical SEO authority

4. **Prepare for "Last Minute" Searches**
   - "last minute christmas gifts"
   - "same day delivery mother's day flowers"
   - Higher commercial intent
