
### Local Keywords

**What It Is:**
Keywords that include geographic locations or have local search intent, targeting users in specific areas.

**Why They're Critical:**
- Less competitive than national keywords
- Higher conversion rates (ready to buy locally)
- Essential for local businesses
- 46% of all Google searches have local intent
- "Near me" searches have grown 900% in recent years

**Types of Local Keywords:**

1. **City/Region Name**
   - "dentist in Seattle"
   - "plumber Portland Oregon"

2. **Neighborhood Specific**
   - "coffee shop in Brooklyn Heights"
   - "yoga studio downtown Austin"

3. **"Near Me" Keywords**
   - "pizza near me"
   - "urgent care near me"

4. **Landmark-Based**
   - "restaurant near Space Needle"
   - "hotel near Times Square"

5. **Area Code/Zip**
   - "electrician 90210"
   - "daycare 60614"

**Local Keyword Research Process:**

**Step 1: List Your Service Areas**
```
Primary City: Austin, TX
Neighborhoods: Zilker, Downtown, South Congress, East Austin, Westlake
Surrounding Areas: Round Rock, Cedar Park, Georgetown, Pflugerville
```

**Step 2: Combine Services with Locations**
```
Services: auto repair, brake repair, transmission, oil change, 
          engine repair, tire service

Formula: [Service] + [Location]

Examples:
- "brake repair Zilker Austin"
- "transmission shop Round Rock TX"
- "oil change near downtown Austin"
- "tire shop Cedar Park"
```

**Step 3: Research Competitors' Local Rankings**
```
Use: BrightLocal, Whitespark, or Google Maps

Find what neighborhoods they rank for:
Competitor A: Strong in North Austin, weak in South
Competitor B: Dominates downtown, absent in suburbs
Your Opportunity: Focus on South Austin & suburbs
```

**Step 4: Use Google My Business Insights**
```
Check how customers find you:
- Direct searches: "XYZ Auto Repair Austin"
- Discovery searches: "auto repair near me"
- Most common search terms
- Where searchers are located
```

**Local Keyword Optimization Checklist:**

**On-Page Elements:**
```
✓ Title Tag: "Best Auto Repair in Austin, TX | XYZ Automotive"
✓ Meta Description: Include city/neighborhood
✓ H1: "Austin's Trusted Auto Repair Shop Since 1998"
✓ Content: Mention location 3-5 times naturally
✓ Address: Schema markup with full address
✓ Local landmarks: "Located near Zilker Park"
✓ Service area map: Show coverage areas
```

**Off-Page Elements:**
```
✓ Google My Business: Fully optimized
✓ NAP Consistency: Same Name, Address, Phone everywhere
✓ Local Citations: Yelp, YellowPages, local directories
✓ Local Backlinks: Chamber of Commerce, local news, blogs
✓ Reviews: Google, Yelp, Facebook with location mentions
```

**"Near Me" Optimization:**

"Near me" searches don't explicitly include location but Google determines it from user's GPS/IP.

**How to Optimize:**
```
1. Strong Google My Business profile
2. Accurate NAP (Name, Address, Phone)
3. Location-specific content on your site
4. Mobile-friendly website (most "near me" searches on mobile)
5. Fast loading speed
6. Click-to-call phone numbers
7. Embedded Google Map
8. Local business schema markup
```

**Local Keyword Variations to Target:**

```
[City] Variations:
- "in [city]"
- "[city] [state]"

- "[city] area"

Neighborhood Variations:

- "[service] near me"

Emergency/Urgent:
```
