### Mixed Intent Example

Some keywords have multiple intents. Google shows different result types to satisfy all users.

**Example Keyword:** "running shoes"

**What Google Shows:**
1. **Transactional:** Shopping ads and product pages (buy running shoes)
2. **Informational:** "Best running shoes 2025" articles
3. **Commercial:** Reviews and comparisons
4. **Navigational:** Nike, Adidas brand pages

**Your Strategy:** Create content matching the SERP (Search Engine Results Page) intent you see.
