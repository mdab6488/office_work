## 3.2 Keyword Optimization


### Keyword Placement

**What It Is:**
Strategically positioning your target keywords in specific locations on your page where Google pays the most attention.

**Why It Matters:**
Where you place keywords signals to Google what your page is about. Strategic placement improves relevance without keyword stuffing.

**Critical Keyword Placement Locations (Priority Order):**

#### 1. Title Tag (Most Important)
**Rules:**
- Include primary keyword near the beginning
- Keep under 60 characters (to avoid truncation)
- Make it compelling for clicks

❌ **Poor Title Tag:**
```
"Everything You Need to Know About Gardening and Growing Plants"
- Generic
- Keyword too far from beginning
- Not specific
```

✅ **Optimized Title Tag:**
```
"How to Grow Tomatoes: Beginner's Guide (8 Simple Steps)"
- Primary keyword ("grow tomatoes") at the start
- Compelling (beginner-friendly, numbered)
- 55 characters
```

#### 2. URL Slug
**Rules:**
- Short and descriptive
- Include primary keyword
- Use hyphens, not underscores
- Lowercase only

❌ **Poor URL:**
```
website.com/blog/post-12345-about-growing-tomatoes-in-your-garden
```

✅ **Optimized URL:**
```
website.com/how-to-grow-tomatoes
```

#### 3. H1 Heading (Page Title)
**Rules:**
- Only one H1 per page
- Should match or closely relate to title tag
- Include primary keyword naturally

❌ **Poor H1:**
```
"Welcome to My Gardening Blog"
```

✅ **Optimized H1:**
```
"How to Grow Tomatoes: Complete Beginner's Guide"
```

#### 4. First 100-150 Words (Introduction)
**Rules:**
- Mention primary keyword in first paragraph
- Sounds natural, not forced
- Answers search intent immediately

✅ **Example:**
```
Growing tomatoes in your home garden is easier than you think. Whether 
you're planting in containers on your balcony or in raised beds, this 
guide will walk you through exactly how to grow tomatoes from seed to 
harvest. By following these 8 steps, you'll be enjoying fresh, homegrown 
tomatoes in about 60-85 days.
```

#### 5. Subheadings (H2, H3)
**Rules:**
- Include primary or related keywords naturally
- Use variations, not exact repeats
- Make headers descriptive

✅ **Example Structure:**
```
H1: How to Grow Tomatoes: Complete Beginner's Guide

H2: When to Plant Tomatoes (By Climate Zone)
H2: Choosing the Best Tomato Varieties for Your Garden
H2: Preparing Soil for Tomato Plants
H3: Ideal Soil pH for Tomatoes
H3: Essential Nutrients for Tomato Growth
H2: Watering Tomatoes: How Much and How Often
H2: Common Tomato Growing Problems (And Solutions)
```

#### 6. Image Alt Text
**Rules:**
- Describe what's in the image
- Include keyword when relevant
- Keep under 125 characters
- Don't keyword stuff

❌ **Poor Alt Text:**
```
alt="tomatoes tomatoes grow tomatoes growing tomatoes"
```

✅ **Good Alt Text:**
```
alt="Hands planting tomato seedling in garden soil"
alt="Ripe red tomatoes growing on vine in backyard garden"
```

#### 7. Meta Description
**Rules:**
- 150-160 characters
- Include primary keyword
- Compelling call-to-action
- Not a direct ranking factor, but affects click-through rate

✅ **Example:**
```
"Learn how to grow tomatoes in 8 easy steps. This beginner-friendly 
guide covers planting, watering, soil prep, and common problems. 
Start your tomato garden today!"
```

#### 8. Throughout Body Content
**Rules:**
- Use keyword naturally 3-6 times per 1,000 words
- Focus on variations and synonyms
- Prioritize readability over keyword density

**Complete Placement Example:**

```
URL: yoursite.com/how-to-grow-tomatoes

Title Tag: How to Grow Tomatoes: 8 Steps for Beginners (2025 Guide)

H1: How to Grow Tomatoes: Complete Beginner's Guide

First Paragraph:
"Growing tomatoes is one of the most rewarding experiences for beginner 
gardeners. Whether you want to plant tomatoes in containers or garden 
beds, this guide will show you exactly how to grow tomatoes successfully..."

[Image with alt text: "Person planting tomato seedlings in raised garden bed"]

H2: When to Plant Tomatoes

Body text mentioning "tomato plants," "growing tomatoes," and variations...

Meta Description: Learn how to grow tomatoes with this simple 8-step guide 
for beginners. Covers planting, soil, watering & harvesting. Start growing 
today!
```
