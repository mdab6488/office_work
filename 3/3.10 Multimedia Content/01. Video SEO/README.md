
### 3.10.1 Video SEO

**Why Video Matters for SEO:**

- Video content increases organic traffic by 157% (Source: Brightcove)
- Users spend 88% more time on websites with video
- Video results appear in 55% of Google searches
- Videos are 50x more likely to rank on first page than text

**4. Thumbnail Design**

