
## Call-to-Action in Meta Descriptions

### Why CTAs Matter

A strong CTA can increase CTR by 15-20%.

---

### Effective CTA Phrases

**Discovery/Learning:**
- "Learn how"
- "Discover"
- "Find out"
- "Explore"
- "See how"

**Action-Oriented:**
- "Get started"
- "Try now"
- "Shop today"
- "Download free"
- "Sign up"
- "Book now"

**Urgency:**
- "Limited time"
- "Today only"
- "Don't miss"
- "Last chance"
- "Hurry"

---

### CTA Examples by Page Type

**E-commerce Product:**
```html
<meta name="description" content="Shop the iPhone 15 Pro at Best Buy. Get free activation, trade-in offers up to $800, and same-day pickup. Order now and save.">
```
**CTA:** "Order now and save"

**Blog Post:**
```html
<meta name="description" content="Want fluffier pancakes? Learn the secret ingredient that makes restaurant-style pancakes at home. Get the recipe plus 5 pro tips inside.">
```
**CTA:** "Get the recipe plus 5 pro tips inside"

**Service Page:**
```html
<meta name="description" content="Book professional house cleaning in Chicago. Same-day service available. 100% satisfaction guarantee or your money back. Get a free quote now.">
```
**CTA:** "Get a free quote now"

**Lead Magnet:**
```html
<meta name="description" content="Download our free social media content calendar template. Plan 30 days of posts in 1 hour. No email required. Access your template instantly.">
```
**CTA:** "Access your template instantly"

### Real-World CTA Examples

**Grammarly (Software):**
```html
<meta name="description" content="Millions trust Grammarly's free writing app to make their messages, documents, and posts clear, mistake-free, and effective. Try it today.">
```
**CTA:** "Try it today"

**Airbnb (Travel):**
```html
<meta name="description" content="Find the perfect beach house rental for your trip to Malibu. Family-friendly vacation homes with ocean views, private pools, and amazing amenities. Book now.">
```
**CTA:** "Book now"

**Neil Patel (Marketing Blog):**
```html
<meta name="description" content="Want more traffic? Learn the SEO strategies that helped me grow NeilPatel.com to 4 million monthly visitors. Get my free SEO checklist.">
```
**CTA:** "Get my free SEO checklist"
