
### Video Title Optimization

Video titles are the MOST important ranking factor for YouTube SEO.

#### Title Best Practices

**Formula: [Keyword] | [Benefit/Hook] | [Modifier]**

**Real-Life Examples:**

1. **Bad Title**: "My Camera Review"
   **Good Title**: "Sony A7IV Review | Best Camera for Content Creators in 2025?"
   - Contains keyword: "Sony A7IV Review"
   - Shows benefit: "Best Camera for Content Creators"
   - Includes year modifier: "2025"

2. **Bad Title**: "Cooking Pasta"
   **Good Title**: "Perfect Creamy Carbonara Recipe | Easy 15-Minute Dinner"
   - Keyword: "Carbonara Recipe"
   - Benefit: "Perfect Creamy"
   - Modifier: "Easy 15-Minute"

3. **Bad Title**: "SEO Tips"
   **Good Title**: "7 SEO Tips That Doubled My Traffic in 30 Days [2025 Guide]"
   - Number (7): Creates curiosity
   - Benefit: "Doubled My Traffic"
   - Timeframe: "30 Days"
   - Recency: "[2025 Guide]"

#### Title Optimization Checklist

```
✅ 60 characters or less (mobile optimization)
✅ Primary keyword at the beginning
✅ Compelling benefit or result
✅ Numbers when possible (lists, stats, timeframes)
✅ Power words (Amazing, Ultimate, Proven, Secret, Insider)
✅ Year/date for evergreen content
✅ Question marks for how-to content
✅ Brackets or pipes for separation
✅ Accurate and not clickbait
```

#### Power Words that Boost CTR

- **Urgency**: Now, Today, Fast, Quick, Instant
- **Value**: Free, Ultimate, Complete, Essential, Proven
- **Emotion**: Amazing, Incredible, Shocking, Stunning
- **Exclusivity**: Secret, Insider, Hidden, Exclusive

**Example Comparison:**

| Poor Title | Optimized Title | Why It Works |
|------------|-----------------|--------------|
| "How to Bake Bread" | "Beginner Bread Baking \| 5-Ingredient No-Knead Recipe" | Targets audience, shows ease, specific number |
| "Digital Marketing" | "Digital Marketing for Beginners \| Complete 2025 Guide" | Targets audience, shows completeness, timely |
| "Yoga Exercises" | "10-Minute Morning Yoga Routine \| Flexibility & Energy Boost" | Specific time, clear benefits |
