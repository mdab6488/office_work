#### Playlist Types

**1. Series Playlists**
```
Example: "30-Day Fitness Challenge"
Day 1: Full Body Workout
Day 2: Upper Body Focus
Day 3: Rest Day Stretching
[...continues to Day 30]
```

**2. Topic Playlists**
```
Example: "Email Marketing Mastery"
- Introduction to Email Marketing
- Building Your Email List
- Writing High-Converting Subject Lines
- Email Automation Strategies
- Analyzing Email Performance
```

**3. Skill Level Playlists**
```
Beginner Photography
Intermediate Photography
Advanced Photography Techniques
```

**4. Format Playlists**
```
Quick Tips (under 5 min)
Full Tutorials (15-30 min)
Live Q&A Sessions
Behind the Scenes
```
