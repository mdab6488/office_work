This appears above "Show More" and in search results.

**Keyword Optimization Example:**

If your video is about "email marketing," naturally include related terms:
- Primary: email marketing
- Secondary: email campaign, newsletter, email automation
- LSI Keywords: open rates, click-through rates, subject lines, segmentation
