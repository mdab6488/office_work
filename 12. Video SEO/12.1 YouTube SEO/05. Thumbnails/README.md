
### Thumbnails

Thumbnails are your billboard. 90% of top-performing videos use custom thumbnails.

#### Thumbnail Best Practices

**Technical Specs:**
```
Resolution: 1280 x 720 pixels (16:9 ratio)
File Size: Under 2MB
Format: JPG, PNG, GIF, or BMP
Safe Zone: Keep text/faces away from edges (20% margin)
```

#### Thumbnail Design Principles

**Real-Life Example: MrBeast**

**What Makes It Work:**
1. **High Contrast Colors**
   - Bright backgrounds (yellow, red, blue)
   - Creates thumb-stopping pattern interrupt

2. **Minimal Text**
   - 3-5 words maximum
   - HUGE, readable font (even on mobile)
   - Example: "I Spent $10,000,000"

3. **Expressive Faces**
   - Close-up human faces with strong emotions
   - Eyes looking at camera or toward text
   - Mouth open (surprise/excitement)

4. **Consistent Branding**
   - Same color scheme across all videos
   - Recognizable style
   - Logo/watermark placement

#### Color Psychology

| Color | Emotion | Use Case |
|-------|---------|----------|
| Red | Urgency, Excitement | News, drama, controversy |
| Yellow | Optimism, Attention | How-tos, positive content |
| Blue | Trust, Calm | Educational, business |
| Green | Growth, Health | Fitness, finance, nature |
| Orange | Energy, Creativity | DIY, creative content |
| Purple | Luxury, Mystery | Premium content, reviews |

#### Thumbnail A/B Testing

**Real-Life Strategy: Veritasium**

Derek Muller tests thumbnails using:
1. **Upload video privately**
2. **Create 3 thumbnail variations**
3. **Run ads to test CTR** (100-500 clicks each)
4. **Choose winner**
5. **Make video public**

**Simple Testing Approach:**
- Week 1: Thumbnail A
- Check CTR in YouTube Analytics
- Week 2: Change to Thumbnail B
- Compare CTR over 2 weeks
- Keep the winner
