
## Key Features

### 1. Discover New Keywords
Find new keyword ideas based on:
- Words or phrases related to your business
- Your website URL
- Competitor websites

### 2. Get Search Volume & Forecasts
See:
- Average monthly searches
- Historical search trends
- Seasonal patterns
- Competition level
- Suggested bid prices for ads

### 3. Filter & Refine
Narrow results by:
- Location (country, city, region)
- Language
- Search networks (Google, search partners)
- Date ranges
- Keyword text filters

### 4. Organize Keywords
- Create keyword plans
- Group keywords by themes
- Export data to CSV or Google Sheets
