
## What is Google Keyword Planner?

Google Keyword Planner is a **free tool** provided by Google Ads (formerly Google AdWords) designed to help advertisers and SEO professionals research keywords for their campaigns. While originally built for paid advertising, it has become an essential tool for:

- **SEO professionals** - Finding organic search opportunities
- **Content creators** - Identifying topics with search demand
- **Digital marketers** - Planning paid search campaigns
- **Business owners** - Understanding customer search behavior
- **Bloggers** - Discovering what their audience is searching for

### Why It Matters

Every day, billions of searches happen on Google. Keyword Planner helps you:
- Discover what people are actually searching for
- Understand search volume trends
- Estimate competition levels
- Find related keywords you might have missed
- Plan your content or advertising budget
