
## Export & Sharing Options

### CSV Export
**What's Included:**
- All keywords/questions
- Search volume (if available)
- CPC data
- Category tags
- Platform source

**Best For:**
- Data analysis in Excel/Google Sheets
- Creating pivot tables
- Filtering and sorting
- Sharing with team members
- Importing to other tools

**Workflow:**
```
Download CSV → Open in Excel → Filter by volume → 
Sort by category → Create content briefs
```

### Image Export (High-Resolution)
**Formats:**
- PNG (high quality)
- JPG (smaller file size)

**Use Cases:**
- Client presentations
- Pitch decks
- Social media posts
- Blog post images
- Internal reports
- Whitepapers

**Pro Tip:** Add your logo/branding to exported images for professional reports.

### Projects & Collections (Pro)
**Features:**
- Group related searches together
- Create folders for different clients
- Organize by topic or campaign
- Save search history
- Quick access to past research

**Example Structure:**
```
├── Client A
│   ├── Product Launch Keywords
│   ├── Blog Content Ideas
│   └── Competitor Analysis
├── Client B
│   ├── SEO Strategy
│   └── Social Media Topics
└── Internal Projects
    ├── Company Blog
    └── Email Campaigns
```

### Share Links
**How It Works:**
- Generate a shareable link to your results
- Anyone with the link can view (read-only)
- No login required for recipients
- Link expires after set time (Pro feature)

**Best For:**
- Collaborating with freelancers
- Sharing with clients
- Getting team feedback
- Presenting to stakeholders
