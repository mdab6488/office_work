- Interactive hover tooltips showing search volume and CPC
- Click to expand specific categories

### 2. **Questions Section**
Displays real questions people ask, organized by question words.

**Question Types:**
- **How**: "How to make cold brew coffee?"
- **What**: "What is the best coffee for espresso?"
- **Why**: "Why does coffee make me tired?"
- **Can**: "Can coffee go bad?"
- **Are**: "Are coffee beans legumes?"
- **Will**: "Will coffee stunt growth?"
- **Should**: "Should coffee be stored in the fridge?"
- **When**: "When was coffee discovered?"
- **Where**: "Where to buy fresh coffee beans?"

**Use Case:** Perfect for creating FAQ pages, blog posts, and tutorial content.

### 3. **Prepositions Section**
Shows context-specific searches using prepositions like "for," "with," "to," "without."

**Real Example:** "Workout"
- "workout **for** beginners"
- "workout **without** equipment"
- "workout **at** home"
- "workout **with** weights"
- "workout **to** lose weight"

**Use Case:** Reveals user intent and specific needs (accessibility, fitness level, available tools).

### 4. **Comparisons Section**
Displays searches where people compare options using "vs," "or," "and," "like."

**Real Example:** "Headphones"
- "noise cancelling headphones **vs** earbuds"
- "wireless **or** wired headphones"
- "headphones **like** AirPods"
- "headphones **and** microphone"

**Use Case:** Create comparison articles, buyer's guides, and product positioning content.

### 5. **Alphabetical Section**
Lists all suggestions organized from A to Z, showing every possible combination.

**Real Example:** "Digital marketing"
- A: "digital marketing **agency**", "digital marketing **analytics**"
- B: "digital marketing **basics**", "digital marketing **bootcamp**"
- C: "digital marketing **courses**", "digital marketing **certification**"

### 6. **People Also Ask (PAA)**
Shows questions from Google's "People Also Ask" feature.

**Real Example:** "SEO"
- "What is SEO and how does it work?"
- "Is SEO worth it in 2025?"
- "How long does SEO take to show results?"
- "What are the 4 types of SEO?"

### 7. **ChatGPT Prompts** (NEW)
Displays the most relevant ChatGPT prompts related to your keyword.

**Includes:**
- Search intent analysis
- Sentiment indicators (positive, negative, neutral)
- Brand mention detection
- Click "Get Answer" for detailed insights

**Real Example:** "Content marketing"
- Prompt: "Create a content marketing strategy for a SaaS startup"
- Intent: Commercial
- Sentiment: Neutral
- Brand Mentioned: No

### 8. **Organic Keywords**
Shows keywords users search for organically on Google and Bing.

**Metrics Provided:**
- **Search Volume**: How often the term is searched
- **CPC (Cost-Per-Click)**: Advertising competitiveness

### 9. **Search Listening Alerts**
Set up notifications for new or trending queries in your niche.

**How It Works:**
- Choose keywords to monitor
- Get weekly email digests
- Track emerging topics
- Detect seasonal trends
- Monitor brand perception shifts

**Real Example:**
- Keyword: "electric vehicles"
- Alert triggers: "electric vehicles **tax credit 2025**"
- Action: Create timely content about new tax incentives

### 10. **Historical Data & Trend Tracking** (Pro)
Compare keyword trends over time to identify patterns.

**Use Cases:**
- Monitor seasonal fluctuations
- Detect surges in demand
- Compare evergreen vs trending topics
- Track competitor mentions
- Forecast content needs
