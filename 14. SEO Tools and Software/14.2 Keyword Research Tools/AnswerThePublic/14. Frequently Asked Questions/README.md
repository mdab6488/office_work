
## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Is AnswerThePublic really free?**  
A: Yes, there's a permanent free plan with 3 searches per day. No credit card required.

**Q: How often is the data updated?**  
A: Autocomplete data is updated in real-time with each search. Search volume and CPC metrics are updated monthly.

**Q: Can I use AnswerThePublic for any language?**  
A: Yes, it supports searches in multiple languages and regions (200+ countries).

**Q: Do I need technical SEO knowledge to use this tool?**  
A: No, it's beginner-friendly. However, SEO knowledge helps you take full advantage of the insights.

**Q: How accurate is the search volume data?**  
A: It's estimated from multiple data providers (similar to other SEO tools). For exact volumes, cross-reference with Google Keyword Planner.

### Pricing & Plans

**Q: Is there a free trial?**  
A: Yes, 7-day free trial available for **monthly plans only** (not lifetime plans).

**Q: What's the difference between monthly and lifetime plans?**  
A: Monthly plans are recurring subscriptions. Lifetime plans are one-time payments with no monthly fees (non-refundable).

**Q: Can I cancel my subscription anytime?**  
A: Yes, monthly subscriptions can be canceled anytime. Lifetime plans are non-refundable.

**Q: What happens when I hit my daily search limit?**  
A: You'll need to wait until the next day (free plan) or upgrade to a paid plan for unlimited searches.

**Q: Is there a student or nonprofit discount?**  
A: Contact support@answerthepublic.com for custom pricing or discounts.

### Features

**Q: Can I save my search results?**  
A: Free users can download CSV/images but can't save within the platform. Pro users can create projects and save searches.

**Q: How do I set up search alerts?**  
A: Available in Pro/Expert plans. Go to your dashboard, select a keyword, and enable "Search Listening Alert" for weekly digests.

**Q: Can I compare data over time?**  
A: Yes, but only in Pro/Expert plans with historical data access.

**Q: How many users can access my account?**  
A: Free/Individual: 1 user | Pro: 3 users | Expert: Unlimited users

**Q: Can I export to other tools?**  
A: Yes, export as CSV and import into Excel, Google Sheets, Ahrefs, SEMrush, or other tools.

### Technical

**Q: Does AnswerThePublic have an API?**  
A: API access is available for Expert plan users upon request. Contact their sales team.

**Q: Can I integrate with Google Analytics or Search Console?**  
A: No direct integration, but you can track content performance separately using those tools.

**Q: Does it work on mobile?**  
A: Yes, the website is mobile-responsive, but desktop experience is better for data analysis.

**Q: Can I white-label reports?**  
A: White-label options are available for Expert/Enterprise plans.

### Use Cases

**Q: Is AnswerThePublic good for local businesses?**  
A: Yes, especially for understanding local search intent and creating location-specific content.

**Q: Can I use it for YouTube SEO?**  
A: Absolutely! Select YouTube as your platform to see video-specific search queries.

**Q: Is it suitable for e-commerce?**  
A: Yes, especially Amazon search data for product research and customer questions.

**Q: Can agencies use it for multiple clients?**  
A: Yes, Pro/Expert plans support multiple projects for organizing client research.

### Comparison

**Q: AnswerThePublic vs Google Keyword Planner?**  
A: AnswerThePublic focuses on question-based searches and visual insights. Google Keyword Planner provides exact search volumes and is better for PPC.

**Q: AnswerThePublic vs Ahrefs?**  
A: Ahrefs is a full SEO suite with backlink analysis and rank tracking. AnswerThePublic specializes in question discovery and content ideation.

**Q: AnswerThePublic vs SEMrush?**  
A: SEMrush offers comprehensive SEO tools. AnswerThePublic is more affordable and focused specifically on search listening and content ideas.

**Q: Do I still need other SEO tools if I have AnswerThePublic?**  
A: Yes, AnswerThePublic is best used alongside other tools. It excels at content ideation but doesn't replace full SEO suites.
