
## Pricing Plans

### **Free Plan**
**Cost:** $0/month

**Includes:**
- 3 searches per day
- Basic visual reports (wheels and lists)
- Limited data export
- Access to all platforms
- Search history (limited)

**Best For:** Casual users, beginners, testing the tool

### **Individual Plan**
**Monthly:** $99/month  
**Lifetime:** $199 (one-time payment)

**Includes:**
- 100 searches per day
- 1 user
- 1 project
- 1 search listening alert
- 25 CSV exports per day
- All visualization options
- Historical data access

**Best For:** Freelancers, solo content creators, small consultants

### **Pro Plan**
**Monthly:** $99-$199/month (exact pricing varies)  
**Lifetime:** Available (check website)

**Includes:**
- Unlimited searches
- Up to 3 users
- Unlimited projects
- 5 search listening alerts
- Unlimited CSV exports
- Compare data over time
- Priority customer support
- High-resolution image exports
- Hide branches & suggestions
- Team collaboration features

**Best For:** Small to mid-sized businesses, marketing teams, agencies

### **Expert Plan**
**Monthly:** $399/month (billed yearly)  
**Lifetime:** Available (check website)

**Includes:**
- Everything in Pro
- Unlimited users
- Advanced analytics
- Custom reporting
- Dedicated account manager
- Training & onboarding
- API access (upon request)
- White-label options

**Best For:** Large enterprises, agencies managing multiple clients

### **7-Day Free Trial**
- Available for **monthly plans only** (not lifetime)
- Full access to all features
- No commitment
- Cancel anytime
- Credit card required
