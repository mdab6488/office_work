
## Getting Started

### Step 1: Create Your Account
1. Go to [AnswerThePublic.com](https://answerthepublic.com)
2. Click "Create Free Account"
3. Sign up with Google, Microsoft, or Email
4. Choose your plan (Free or Trial)

### Step 2: Run Your First Search
1. Enter 1-2 keywords in the search bar
2. Select your target language
3. Choose your geographic region
4. Select platform (Google, Bing, YouTube, etc.)
5. Click "Search"

### Step 3: Explore the Results
- **Wait 10-20 seconds** for data compilation
- Review the visual wheel
- Switch between Lists and Tables views
- Apply filters if needed
- Export data for later use

### Step 4: Take Action
1. Download CSV or save as image
2. Organize results by theme
3. Create content briefs
4. Plan your content calendar
5. Track performance
