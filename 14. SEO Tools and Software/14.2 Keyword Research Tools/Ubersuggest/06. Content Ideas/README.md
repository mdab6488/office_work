
## Content Ideas

### Finding Content That Performs

#### Step 1: Navigate to Content Ideas
- Enter a keyword or topic
- Click "Content Ideas" from the left menu

#### Step 2: Analyze Results
Ubersuggest shows:
- **Articles**: URLs of top-performing content
- **Estimated Visits**: Predicted monthly traffic
- **Social Shares**: Facebook and Pinterest shares
- **Backlinks**: Number of referring domains

### Real-Life Example 3: Content Marketing Strategy

**Scenario**: Digital marketing agency creating blog content.

**Process**:
1. Enter "email marketing" in Content Ideas
2. Top result shows:
   - Title: "Email Marketing Best Practices for 2024"
   - Est. Visits: 15,000/month
   - Social Shares: 2,400
   - Backlinks: 187 domains

3. Click on the article to see:
   - **Top Keywords**: What keywords drive traffic
   - **Backlink Sources**: Who links to this content
   - **Social Engagement**: Which platforms perform best

**Action**: Create similar content covering missing angles or updated information, then reach out to backlink sources for your version.

### Content Gap Analysis

Compare multiple URLs to find content opportunities:
1. Enter your competitor's URL
2. See what content performs for them
3. Identify topics you haven't covered
