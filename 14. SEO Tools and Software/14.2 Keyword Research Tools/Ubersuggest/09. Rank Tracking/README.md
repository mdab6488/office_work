
## Rank Tracking

### Setting Up Rank Tracking

#### Step 1: Add Keywords to Track
1. In your project, click "Tracked Keywords"
2. Add keywords manually or import from CSV
3. Set location preferences

#### Step 2: Monitor Rankings
- **Current Position**: Where you rank today
- **Change**: Position change from previous check
- **Search Volume**: Monthly searches
- **Estimated Visits**: Predicted traffic if you rank #1

### Real-Life Example 7: Tracking Blog Growth

**Scenario**: Personal finance blog tracking keyword performance.

**Process**:
1. Added 50 target keywords to track
2. Initial rankings (Month 1):
   - "budget app" - Position 47
   - "how to save money" - Position 23
   - "best savings account" - Position 15
   - "investment strategies" - Not in top 100

3. After content optimization (Month 4):
   - "budget app" - Position 12 (↑35)
   - "how to save money" - Position 8 (↑15)
   - "best savings account" - Position 4 (↑11)
   - "investment strategies" - Position 67 (NEW)

**Insights**:
- Content updates improved "best savings account" significantly
- "Budget app" needs more backlinks to reach page 1
- "Investment strategies" article finally ranked after building 5 quality backlinks

### Advanced Rank Tracking Tips

1. **Track Competitor Rankings**: Compare your position to competitors
2. **Local Rankings**: Set specific city/region for local SEO
3. **Historical Data**: View ranking changes over time
4. **SERP Features**: See if keywords show featured snippets
