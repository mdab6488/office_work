
## Best Practices

### 1. Keyword Research Best Practices

**Do:**
- ✅ Start with broad seed keywords, then go specific
- ✅ Target keywords with search intent matching your content
- ✅ Mix head terms and long-tail keywords
- ✅ Consider keyword difficulty relative to your domain authority
- ✅ Look for question-based keywords
- ✅ Group related keywords into topic clusters

**Don't:**
- ❌ Only chase high-volume keywords
- ❌ Ignore search intent
- ❌ Stuff keywords unnaturally
- ❌ Target keywords too competitive for your site

### 2. Content Strategy Best Practices

**Successful Content Formula**:
1. Find keyword with decent volume (1,000+ searches)
2. Check SD score is achievable for your domain
3. Analyze top 10 results with Content Ideas
4. Create content that's 10x better:
   - More comprehensive
   - Better formatted
   - More examples
   - Better visuals
   - More up-to-date

### 3. Backlink Building Best Practices

**Quality over Quantity**:
- Target domains with DS > 30
- Prioritize relevant, niche-specific sites
- Seek editorial, contextual links
- Avoid spammy link schemes
- Build relationships, not just links

**Link Building Methods**:
1. **Resource page outreach**: Find "resources" pages in your niche
2. **Broken link building**: Find broken links, suggest your content
3. **Guest posting**: Write valuable content for other sites
4. **Digital PR**: Create newsworthy content
5. **Unlinked mentions**: Find brand mentions without links, request links

### 4. Site Audit Best Practices

**Regular Maintenance Schedule**:
- **Weekly**: Check for new broken links
- **Monthly**: Review critical errors
- **Quarterly**: Full site audit
- **Yearly**: Comprehensive SEO strategy review

**Priority Order**:
1. Fix critical errors (broken links, severe technical issues)
2. Address warnings (missing meta tags, slow pages)
3. Implement recommendations (content improvements, internal linking)

### 5. Rank Tracking Best Practices

**What to Track**:
- Primary target keywords (10-20)
- Brand keywords
- Competitor comparison keywords
- Long-tail content keywords (50-100)
- Local keywords (if applicable)

**How to Use Data**:
- Identify declining rankings → investigate and fix
- Spot opportunities → pages ranking #11-20 need minor optimization
- Track seasonal trends
- Measure impact of SEO changes
