
## Key Features Overview

### 1. **Keyword Research**
Find profitable keywords with search volume, competition, and CPC data.

### 2. **Domain Overview**
Analyze any website's organic traffic, top pages, and keywords.

### 3. **Content Ideas**
Discover popular content in your niche based on social shares and backlinks.

### 4. **Backlink Data**
Explore backlink profiles of any domain.

### 5. **Site Audit**
Identify technical SEO issues on your website.

### 6. **Rank Tracking**
Monitor your keyword rankings over time.

### 7. **SEO Analyzer**
Get on-page SEO recommendations for specific pages.
