
## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: Is KeywordTool.io free?

**A:** Yes and no. 
- Free version: Generates keyword ideas but no metrics
- Paid version: Required for search volume, CPC, and competition data
- Free version is useful for brainstorming, but you need Pro for serious SEO

---

### Q2: How accurate is KeywordTool.io data?

**A:** Generally accurate but not perfect.
- Uses real Google Autocomplete data (100% accurate)
- Search volume estimates may differ from Google Keyword Planner
- CPC data is based on Google Ads historical data
- Some keywords may show 0 volume but still get traffic

**Recommendation:** Use as one of multiple tools for cross-verification.

---

### Q3: Can I use KeywordTool.io without signing up?

**A:** Yes for free version!
- No account needed
- Instant access
- No credit card required
- But features are limited

---

### Q4: Does KeywordTool.io work for YouTube?

**A:** Yes!
- Dedicated YouTube keyword research
- Up to 25 keywords per search term
- Shows what people search on YouTube specifically
- Perfect for content creators

---

### Q5: What's better: KeywordTool.io or Google Keyword Planner?

**A:** Both have strengths.

**KeywordTool.io is better for:**
- Long-tail keyword discovery
- Question-based keywords
- No Google Ads account needed
- Multi-platform research

**Google Keyword Planner is better for:**
- More accurate data
- PPC campaign planning
- Integration with Google Ads
- It's free

**Best strategy:** Use both together!

### Q12: How is this different from Ubersuggest or SEMrush?

**A:**

**KeywordTool.io:**
- Focused on keyword generation
- Uses Autocomplete
- Multi-platform
- No additional SEO tools

**Ubersuggest:**
- All-in-one SEO tool
- Includes site audit, backlinks
- More affordable
- Less keywords per search

**SEMrush:**
- Comprehensive SEO suite
- Includes everything (audit, rank tracking, backlinks)
- Most expensive
- Best for agencies

**Choose based on needs and budget.**

### Q14: Is it worth it for a beginner?

**A:** Depends on your budget.

**Worth it if:**
- You're serious about SEO
- You create content regularly
- You need long-tail keywords
- You can afford $69-89/month

**Not worth it if:**
- You're just starting out
- You have a tiny budget
- You only need occasional research
- Free tools like Ubersuggest meet your needs

**Alternative:** Start with free version + Google Keyword Planner, upgrade later.

---

### Q15: Can I find competitor keywords?

**A:** Indirectly, yes.
- You can't input competitor URL to extract their keywords
- But you can research topics they cover
- Identify gaps in their content
- Find opportunities they missed

For direct competitor keyword extraction, use SEMrush or Ahrefs.
