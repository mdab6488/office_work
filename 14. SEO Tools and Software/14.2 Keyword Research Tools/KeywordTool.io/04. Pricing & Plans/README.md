
## Pricing & Plans

### Free Version

**Cost:** $0/month

**What you get:**
- Up to 750+ keyword suggestions per search
- Basic keyword list (no metrics)
- Limited searches per day
- No account registration required

**Limitations:**
- No search volume data
- No CPC data
- No competition metrics
- No trend data
- Limited export options

**Best for:** Beginners exploring keyword research or generating quick content ideas.

---

### Pro Basic

**Cost:** 
- **$89/month** (billed monthly)
- **$69/month** (billed annually - save 22%)

**What you get:**
- All Free features
- Search volume data (average + 12-month history)
- Trend charts
- More keyword suggestions
- Bulk search volume analysis
- **1 user account**
- Export to Excel/CSV
- Priority support

**Best for:** Individual bloggers, freelancers, or solo entrepreneurs doing SEO for 1-2 websites.

---

**Cost:**
- **$99/month** (billed monthly)

- **Up to 5 user accounts**

- Priority support

**Best for:** Small to medium-sized teams, SEO agencies, or businesses managing multiple websites.
