### ❌ Cons

**1. Limited Free Version**
- No search volume data
- No CPC data
- No competition metrics
- Can't make informed decisions without data

**2. Expensive Pricing**
- Starting at $89/month
- More expensive than some alternatives
- No monthly free trial
- Annual commitment for best price

**3. Keyword Research Only**
- No site audits
- No backlink analysis
- No rank tracking
- No content optimization tools

**4. Limited Competitor Analysis**
- Can't see competitors' ranking keywords directly
- No SERP analysis
- No keyword difficulty score

**5. No Keyword Difficulty Metric**
- Must assess competition manually
- No clear "easy vs hard" indicator
- Requires additional tools

**6. Cancellation Issues**
- Some users report difficulty canceling
- Must email support for refunds
- Not instant cancellation

**7. Data Accuracy Concerns**
- Sometimes shows 0 search volume for keywords that do get traffic
- Estimates may vary from Google Keyword Planner
- Not always 100% accurate

**8. Limited Integration**
- No native WordPress plugin
- No Shopify app
- Manual export required

**9. No Content Optimization**
- Can't optimize existing content
- No on-page SEO suggestions
- Just provides keywords

**10. Learning Curve for Full Value**
- Free version too limited
- Must invest in Pro to make informed decisions
- Requires understanding of SEO principles
