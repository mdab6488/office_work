
## Real-Life Examples & Use Cases

### Example 1: E-commerce Product Research

**Scenario:** You sell handmade organic soap and want to optimize your Shopify store.

**Process:**
1. Enter seed keyword: "handmade soap"
2. Select: Google, United States, English
3. Click Search

**Results you might get:**
- organic handmade soap
- handmade soap benefits
- best handmade soap
- handmade soap for sensitive skin
- handmade soap vs store bought
- how to make handmade soap
- handmade soap without lye
- natural handmade soap near me
- handmade soap for acne
- lavender handmade soap

**Actionable insights:**
- Create product pages for "organic handmade soap" and "handmade soap for sensitive skin"
- Write blog post: "Handmade Soap Benefits: Why Switch from Store-Bought"
- Create FAQ page answering "handmade soap without lye"
- Optimize for local SEO: "natural handmade soap near me"
- Develop new product line: "lavender handmade soap" (if not already offered)

**SEO Strategy:**
- High-volume keywords: "handmade soap", "organic handmade soap"
- Long-tail keywords (easier to rank): "handmade soap for acne", "handmade soap without lye"
- Content keywords: "handmade soap benefits", "how to make handmade soap"

### Example 6: Google Ads Campaign

**Scenario:** You offer online graphic design courses.

**Process:**
1. Enter seed keyword: "graphic design course"
2. Enable CPC data (Pro Plus required)
3. Analyze competition and cost

**Example data:**

| Keyword | Search Volume | CPC | Competition |
|---------|--------------|-----|-------------|
| graphic design course | 8,100 | $4.50 | High |
| online graphic design course | 2,900 | $3.20 | Medium |
| free graphic design course | 1,600 | $1.80 | Low |
| graphic design course for beginners | 1,300 | $2.90 | Medium |
| adobe graphic design course | 880 | $5.20 | High |
| graphic design certificate course | 720 | $3.80 | Medium |

**Campaign structure:**

**High-Intent Campaign** (Higher budget)
- Keywords: "graphic design certificate course", "adobe graphic design course"
- CPC bid: $4-6
- Landing page: Course enrollment page
- Goal: Direct conversions

**Mid-Funnel Campaign** (Medium budget)
- Keywords: "online graphic design course", "graphic design course for beginners"
- CPC bid: $2.50-3.50
- Landing page: Course overview + testimonials
- Goal: Lead generation

**Awareness Campaign** (Lower budget)
- Keywords: "free graphic design course"
- CPC bid: $1.50-2
- Landing page: Free intro course + upsell
- Goal: List building

**ROI calculation:**
- If course costs $199
- Average CPC: $3
- If conversion rate is 5%
- Cost per conversion: $3 ÷ 0.05 = $60
- Profit per sale: $199 - $60 = $139

### Example 7: Competitor Analysis

**Scenario:** You want to understand what keywords your competitors are targeting.

**Process:**
1. Visit competitor's website
2. Note their main topics/products
3. Use KeywordTool.io to research those topics
4. Compare with your own content

**Example:**

**Competitor:** Sells "matcha tea"

**Your research:**
- best matcha tea
- organic matcha tea
- matcha tea benefits
- matcha tea powder
- how to make matcha tea
- ceremonial grade matcha
- matcha tea recipes
- matcha tea vs green tea

**Gap analysis:**

**Competitor has content on:**
- "best matcha tea" ✓
- "matcha tea benefits" ✓
- "how to make matcha tea" ✓

**Competitor missing:**
- "matcha tea vs green tea" ✗
- "ceremonial grade matcha" ✗
- "matcha tea recipes" ✗

**Your strategy:**
Create content for the gaps:
1. "Matcha Tea vs Green Tea: Which is Better for You?"
2. "What is Ceremonial Grade Matcha? The Ultimate Guide"
3. "15 Delicious Matcha Tea Recipes to Try Today"

This gives you opportunities to outrank competitors on topics they haven't covered.
