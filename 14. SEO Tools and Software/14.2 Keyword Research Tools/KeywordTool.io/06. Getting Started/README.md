
## Getting Started

### Step 1: Access KeywordTool.io

Visit: https://keywordtool.io

**No account required** for free version!

---

### Step 2: Choose Your Platform

Select from the platform dropdown:
- Google (default)
- YouTube
- Bing
- Amazon
- eBay
- Instagram
- Twitter
- Play Store
- App Store

---

### Step 3: Enter Your Seed Keyword

**Best practices for seed keywords:**
- Use 1-2 words for broad research
- Be specific for niche topics
- Avoid overly generic terms initially
- Use your product/service name

**Examples:**
- Good: "yoga mat", "running shoes", "vegan recipes"
- Too broad: "fitness", "shoes", "food"

---

### Step 4: Select Location & Language

- Choose your **target country**
- Select your **target language**
- This ensures localized results

**Example:** 
- Google domain: United States
- Language: English

---

### Step 5: Click Search

The tool will generate:
- **Questions** - Keywords starting with question words
- **Prepositions** - Keywords with prepositions
- **Alphabetical** - Keywords sorted A-Z
- **Related** - Semantically related terms

---

### Step 6: Analyze Results

**Free version shows:**
- List of keyword suggestions
- Basic keyword structure

**Pro version shows:**
- Search volume (average monthly)
- Trend data (12-month chart)
- CPC data (Pro Plus & Business)
- Competition level (Pro Plus & Business)

---

### Step 7: Export or Use Keywords

**Options:**
- Copy to clipboard
- Export to Excel
- Export to CSV
- Add to Keyword Basket for comparison
