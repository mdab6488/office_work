## Introduction

Clearscope is an AI-powered SEO content optimization platform that revolutionizes how content creators, marketers, and SEO professionals produce search-optimized content. Founded in 2016 by Bernard Huang and Kevin Su, Clearscope has become one of the most trusted tools for data-driven content optimization, serving companies like IBM, HubSpot, Adobe, and thousands of content teams worldwide.
