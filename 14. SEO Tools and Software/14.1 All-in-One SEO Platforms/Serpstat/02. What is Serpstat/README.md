
## What is Serpstat?

Serpstat is an all-in-one SEO platform that provides comprehensive tools for:

- **Keyword Research** - Discover profitable keywords with search volume, difficulty, and CPC data
- **Competitor Analysis** - Spy on competitors' SEO and PPC strategies
- **Rank Tracking** - Monitor your keyword positions across search engines
- **Site Auditing** - Identify and fix technical SEO issues
- **Backlink Analysis** - Analyze link profiles and build better backlinks
- **Content Marketing** - Generate AI-powered content and analyze text quality
- **PPC Research** - Optimize paid advertising campaigns

### Why Serpstat?

✅ **Affordable** - Starting at $59/month (much cheaper than Ahrefs/SEMrush)  
✅ **User-Friendly** - Intuitive interface suitable for beginners and experts  
✅ **Comprehensive** - 50+ tools in one platform  
✅ **Accurate Data** - Reliable keyword and ranking data from 230 countries  
✅ **API Access** - Automate workflows even on lower-tier plans  
✅ **White-Label Reports** - Perfect for agencies managing multiple clients
