### Troubleshooting

**Q: My daily search limit ran out. What now?**  
A:
1. Wait until tomorrow (limits reset at midnight UTC)
2. Upgrade to higher plan (more searches)
3. Use filters to get more specific results (fewer searches needed)
4. Export data for offline analysis

Pro tip: Plan your research to use searches efficiently.

---

**Q: Why don't I see data for my keyword?**  
A: Possible reasons:
- Very low search volume (<10/month) - Serpstat filters these
- New keyword - not in database yet
- Misspelled - check spelling
- Wrong database selected - choose correct country
- Can request data addition (takes ~1 week)

---

**Q: The site audit found too many issues. Where do I start?**  
A: Focus on this priority order:
1. **Critical (Red)** issues first
2. High-traffic pages
3. Quick wins (easy fixes with big impact)
4. Work down to medium and low priority

Don't try to fix everything at once!
