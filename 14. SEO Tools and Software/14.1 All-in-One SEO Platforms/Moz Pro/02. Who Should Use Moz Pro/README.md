
## Who Should Use Moz Pro?

### Perfect For:
✅ **Small Business Owners** - Managing their own website's SEO  
✅ **Digital Marketers** - Running SEO campaigns for companies  
✅ **SEO Consultants** - Providing professional SEO services  
✅ **Marketing Agencies** - Managing multiple client accounts  
✅ **Content Creators** - Optimizing blog posts and articles  
✅ **E-commerce Businesses** - Improving product page rankings  
✅ **In-House Marketing Teams** - Coordinating SEO strategies  

### Not Ideal For:
❌ Users needing real-time, minute-by-minute data updates  
❌ Large enterprises requiring massive keyword databases (100k+ keywords)  
❌ Users who need the most advanced technical SEO features  
❌ Those on very tight budgets (under $50/month)
