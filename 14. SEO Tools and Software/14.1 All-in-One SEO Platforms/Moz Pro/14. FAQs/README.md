
## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: Does Moz Pro offer a free trial?**  
A: Yes, 30 days free for all plans. No credit card required in most regions.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**  
A: Yes, cancel anytime. No long-term contracts. If you cancel mid-month, access continues until billing period ends.

**Q: Is there a discount for annual billing?**  
A: Yes, save 20-25% by paying annually instead of monthly.

**Q: How many websites can I track?**  
A: Depends on plan—3 campaigns (Starter) to 100 campaigns (Premium).

**Q: Can I change plans later?**  
A: Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime. Prorated billing adjustments.

### Technical Questions

**Q: How often does Moz update rankings?**  
A: Weekly by default. Some competitors offer daily, but weekly is sufficient for most SEO work.

**Q: What's a good Domain Authority score?**  
A: 
- 0-20: New/weak site
- 20-40: Developing site
- 40-60: Established site
- 60-80: Authoritative site
- 80-100: Extremely authoritative (think Wikipedia, BBC, etc.)

**Q: How accurate is Moz's keyword difficulty score?**  
A: Generally accurate, but remember it's an estimate. Factors: your DA, content quality, and backlinks matter more than the score.

**Q: Can Moz track international keywords?**  
A: Yes, supports 200+ countries and all major search engines (Google, Bing, Yahoo, etc.).

**Q: How large of a site can Moz crawl?**  
A: Up to 10 million pages on Premium plan. Typical sites (under 10,000 pages) work great on Standard plan.

### Comparison Questions

**Q: Should I choose Moz or Ahrefs?**  
A: 
- Choose Moz: Beginner-friendly, better price, focused on core SEO
- Choose Ahrefs: Need largest backlink index, daily updates, advanced features

**Q: Should I choose Moz or SEMrush?**  
A:
- Choose Moz: Pure SEO, cleaner interface, better for small teams
- Choose SEMrush: Need PPC tools, social media, content marketing features too

**Q: Can I use Moz and another tool together?**  
A: Yes! Many SEO pros use Moz for some features and supplement with other tools. Common combo: Moz + Google Search Console.

### Pricing Questions

**Q: Is Moz Pro worth the money?**  
A: If SEO is important to your business, yes. ROI typically positive within 3-6 months. A $79/month plan that helps you rank better and get more organic traffic easily pays for itself.

**Q: Are there any hidden fees?**  
A: No hidden fees. Additional costs only if you add: extra users ($49/user), Moz Local (separate product), or exceed campaign limits.

**Q: Do nonprofits get discounts?**  
A: Contact Moz sales—they occasionally offer nonprofit discounts on case-by-case basis.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**  
A: 30-day money-back guarantee if not satisfied.

### Feature Questions

**Q: Can Moz track local rankings (e.g., "pizza near me")?**  
A: Yes, set location to specific city or zip code when setting up campaign.

**Q: Does Moz integrate with Google Analytics?**  
A: Yes, seamless integration to see traffic data alongside rankings.

**Q: Can I track competitors?**  
A: Yes, add up to 3 competitors per campaign (varies by plan).

**Q: Does Moz have an API?**  
A: Yes, available on Medium, Large, and Premium plans.
