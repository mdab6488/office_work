### 1. **Keyword Explorer** ðŸ”

The heart of Moz Pro's keyword research capabilities.

#### What It Does:
- Discovers valuable keywords with high ranking potential
- Provides monthly search volume estimates
- Calculates keyword difficulty scores (0-100)
- Shows organic click-through rate (CTR) estimates
- Suggests related keywords and long-tail variations
- Displays SERP feature opportunities (featured snippets, etc.)

#### Real-Life Example:
```
Scenario: You run a coffee shop blog and want to rank for coffee-related terms.

Step 1: Enter "cold brew coffee" in Keyword Explorer
Results:
- Monthly Volume: 74,000 searches
- Difficulty Score: 58/100 (Moderate)
- Organic CTR: 42%
- Priority Score: 68 (Recommended!)

Step 2: Check related keywords
- "how to make cold brew coffee" - 27,000 searches, Difficulty: 45
- "cold brew coffee recipe" - 18,000 searches, Difficulty: 42
- "best cold brew coffee" - 12,000 searches, Difficulty: 51

Decision: Target "how to make cold brew coffee" first (lower difficulty, good volume)
```

#### Key Metrics Explained:
- **Search Volume:** Monthly average searches
- **Difficulty (1-100):** How hard to rank (competitive analysis)
- **Organic CTR:** Percentage of searchers who click organic results
- **Priority Score:** Moz's recommendation combining volume, difficulty, and CTR

### 3. **Site Crawl (Site Audit)** ðŸ•·ï¸

Comprehensive technical SEO audit tool that scans your website for issues.

#### What It Does:
- Crawls up to 5 million pages (depending on plan)
- Identifies broken links (404 errors)
- Finds duplicate content issues
- Detects missing or duplicate meta titles/descriptions
- Checks for slow-loading pages

- Identifies thin content (pages with little text)
- Checks for missing alt tags on images

#### Real-Life Example:
```

   - 89 pages with thin content (<300 words)

Week 1: Fix all critical issues (duplicate titles, broken links)

Week 3: Improve page speed on slow pages
Week 4: Expand thin content pages

Result: After fixes, organic traffic increased 34% in 2 months
```

#### Crawl Schedule:
- **Weekly Crawls:** Automatically scan your site every week
- **On-Demand Crawls:** Run manual crawls when making site changes
- **Custom Crawls:** Focus on specific sections of your site
