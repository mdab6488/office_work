
## What is Moz Pro?

**Moz Pro** is a comprehensive all-in-one SEO (Search Engine Optimization) software platform designed to help businesses, marketers, and agencies improve their search engine visibility and drive qualified organic traffic to their websites.

### Key Highlights:
- **Founded:** By Moz (formerly SEOmoz) in Seattle, Washington
- **Experience:** Over 20 years of SEO expertise
- **Target Users:** Small businesses, mid-sized companies, agencies, and enterprises
- **Primary Purpose:** Keyword research, rank tracking, site audits, backlink analysis, and competitive research
- **Update Frequency:** 2025 version includes AI-powered features

### What Makes Moz Pro Unique?
- **Domain Authority (DA):** Proprietary metric (0-100 scale) that predicts ranking potential
- **Page Authority (PA):** Similar metric for individual pages
- **Beginner-Friendly:** Known for its intuitive interface and educational content
- **Community-Driven:** Strong support through Moz Blog, Whiteboard Friday videos, and Moz Academy
