
## Moz vs Competitors

### Moz Pro vs Ahrefs

| Feature | Moz Pro | Ahrefs | Winner |
|---------|---------|--------|--------|
| **Pricing** | $99-599/month | $129-999/month | Moz (cheaper) |
| **Backlink Index** | 44 billion | 36 trillion | Ahrefs (much larger) |
| **Keyword Database** | 1.25 billion | 10 billion | Ahrefs |
| **User Interface** | Very beginner-friendly | More complex | Moz |
| **Site Audit** | Excellent | Excellent | Tie |
| **Domain Authority** | Yes (proprietary) | Yes (DR) | Tie |
| **Update Frequency** | Weekly | Daily | Ahrefs |
| **Best For** | Beginners, small agencies | Power users, large agencies | Depends on needs |

**Bottom Line:** Choose Moz if you want ease-of-use and affordability. Choose Ahrefs if you need the most comprehensive data.

### Moz Pro vs SEMrush

| Feature | Moz Pro | SEMrush | Winner |
|---------|---------|---------|--------|
| **Pricing** | $99-599/month | $130-500/month | Moz (slightly cheaper) |
| **Keyword Research** | Good | Excellent | SEMrush |
| **Rank Tracking** | Good | Excellent | SEMrush |
| **Content Marketing** | Basic | Advanced (AI writing) | SEMrush |
| **Social Media Tools** | No | Yes | SEMrush |
| **PPC Tools** | No | Yes (extensive) | SEMrush |
| **Learning Curve** | Easy | Moderate | Moz |
| **Best For** | Pure SEO | All-in-one marketing | Depends on needs |

**Bottom Line:** Choose Moz for focused SEO. Choose SEMrush if you need PPC, social, and content tools too.

### Moz Pro vs Ubersuggest

| Feature | Moz Pro | Ubersuggest | Winner |

| **Backlink Analysis** | Excellent | Good | Moz |
| **Site Audit** | Excellent | Basic | Moz |
| **Learning Curve** | Easy | Very easy | Ubersuggest |
| **Best For** | Serious SEO | Casual users, tight budgets | Depends on budget |

**Bottom Line:** Choose Moz for professional SEO work. Choose Ubersuggest if budget is extremely limited.
