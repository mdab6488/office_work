
## Competitor Analysis

### Organic Research Tool

Identify which keywords your competitors rank for and find gaps in your strategy.

### Real-Life Example 5: Finding Competitor Weaknesses

**Scenario**: You run a local bakery website competing with `kingarthurbaking.com`

**Analysis Process**:

1. **Enter Competitor Domain**: `kingarthurbaking.com`

2. **Top Organic Keywords**:
```
- "sourdough bread recipe" - Position 1, 301K searches
- "chocolate chip cookie recipe" - Position 2, 246K searches
- "pizza dough recipe" - Position 3, 165K searches
- "how to make bread" - Position 1, 110K searches
```

3. **Use Keyword Gap Tool**:
   - Your domain: `mylocalbakery.com`
   - Competitor: `kingarthurbaking.com`

4. **Gap Analysis Results**:
```
Keywords Competitor Has, You Don't:
- "gluten free bread recipe" - 89K searches, Difficulty: 52%
- "vegan cookie recipe" - 67K searches, Difficulty: 48%
- "artisan bread techniques" - 12K searches, Difficulty: 35%

Keywords You Both Have (Comparison):
- "croissant recipe" - You: Position 15, Them: Position 3
- "cake decorating tips" - You: Position 22, Them: Position 5

Keywords You Have, They Don't:
- "local bakery wedding cakes" - 3.2K searches, Position 4
```

**Strategic Actions**:
- Create content for gluten-free and vegan recipes (untapped opportunity)
- Improve existing content for "croissant recipe" (close the gap)
- Double down on local keywords where you're winning

### Real-Life Example 6: Traffic Analytics (Competitive Benchmark)

**Scenario**: Compare your SaaS product site with competitors

**Traffic Analysis Dashboard**:
```
Your Site: projectmanagement-tool.com
Monthly Visits: 45K

Competitor A: asana.com
Monthly Visits: 12.5M
Traffic Sources:
  - Direct: 45%
  - Organic: 35%
  - Paid: 8%
  - Referral: 7%
  - Social: 5%

Competitor B: trello.com
Monthly Visits: 8.3M
Traffic Sources:
  - Direct: 50%
  - Organic: 30%
  - Paid: 5%
  - Referral: 10%
  - Social: 5%
```

**Key Insights**:
- Competitors get 45-50% direct traffic (strong brand awareness)
- Organic traffic is 30-35% (SEO is crucial)
- You're at 45K vs millions - need aggressive content + link building strategy
- Focus on referral partnerships (competitors get 7-10%)
