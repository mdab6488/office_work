
## Position Tracking

### Monitoring Your Keyword Rankings

Position Tracking lets you monitor your rankings for specific keywords over time.

### Real-Life Example 8: Local Business Ranking Campaign

**Scenario**: You own a dental practice and want to rank for local keywords in Austin, Texas

**Campaign Setup**:
```
Domain: austinfamilydental.com
Location: Austin, Texas, USA
Device: Desktop + Mobile

Target Keywords (20):
1. "dentist austin tx" - Current: Not in top 100
2. "family dentist austin" - Current: Position 45
3. "teeth whitening austin" - Current: Position 18
4. "emergency dentist austin" - Current: Not in top 100
5. "cosmetic dentistry austin" - Current: Position 32
6. "austin dental implants" - Current: Position 28
7. "pediatric dentist austin" - Current: Position 15
8. "invisalign austin" - Current: Position 22
9. "austin teeth cleaning" - Current: Position 12
10. "root canal austin tx" - Current: Position 38
```

**After 6 Months of SEO Work**:
```
Position Changes:
1. "dentist austin tx" - Not ranking → Position 8 ⬆️ (+92)
2. "family dentist austin" - Position 45 → Position 6 ⬆️ (+39)
3. "teeth whitening austin" - Position 18 → Position 3 ⬆️ (+15)
4. "emergency dentist austin" - Not ranking → Position 15 ⬆️ (+85)
5. "cosmetic dentistry austin" - Position 32 → Position 9 ⬆️ (+23)
6. "austin dental implants" - Position 28 → Position 5 ⬆️ (+23)
7. "pediatric dentist austin" - Position 15 → Position 4 ⬆️ (+11)
8. "invisalign austin" - Position 22 → Position 7 ⬆️ (+15)
9. "austin teeth cleaning" - Position 12 → Position 2 ⬆️ (+10)
10. "root canal austin tx" - Position 38 → Position 11 ⬆️ (+27)

Average Visibility: +245%
Organic Traffic: +320% (from 450 to 1,890 monthly visits)
New Patient Calls: +180%
```

**What Actions Led to These Results**:
- Created 15 location-specific blog posts
- Optimized Google Business Profile
- Built 45 local citations (Yelp, Healthgrades, etc.)
- Earned 12 local backlinks from Austin community sites
- Added service-specific landing pages
- Collected 67 new patient reviews
