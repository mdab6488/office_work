### Real-Life Example 3: Starting a New Blog

**Scenario**: You're starting a travel blog and need to find profitable keywords

**Process**:

1. **Seed Keyword Entry**: Enter `travel tips`

2. **Results Analysis**:
```
"travel tips" - 90.5K monthly searches, Difficulty: 72%, CPC: $1.23

Related Keywords (sorted by volume):
- "budget travel tips" - 22K searches, Difficulty: 45%, CPC: $0.89
- "solo travel tips" - 18K searches, Difficulty: 42%, CPC: $0.95
- "travel tips for europe" - 14K searches, Difficulty: 38%, CPC: $1.10
- "travel packing tips" - 12K searches, Difficulty: 35%, CPC: $0.67
- "family travel tips" - 8.9K searches, Difficulty: 40%, CPC: $1.05
```

3. **Filter Application**:
   - Keyword Difficulty: 0-40% (easier to rank)
   - Search Volume: 1,000+ (worth the effort)
   - Word Count: 3-4 words (long-tail keywords)

4. **Golden Opportunities Found**:
```
- "solo female travel tips asia" - 2.4K searches, Difficulty: 28%
- "budget backpacking europe tips" - 3.1K searches, Difficulty: 32%
- "first time travel tips japan" - 1.8K searches, Difficulty: 25%
```

**Why These Are Great**:
- Lower competition (easier to rank)
- Specific intent (people know what they want)
- Good search volume (enough traffic potential)

### Real-Life Example 4: E-commerce Keyword Research

**Scenario**: You sell organic skincare products online

**Product-Focused Research**:

1. **Seed Keyword**: `organic face serum`

2. **Buyer Intent Keywords**:
```
Informational (Top of Funnel):
- "benefits of organic face serum" - 4.2K, Difficulty: 35%
- "what is organic face serum" - 1.8K, Difficulty: 20%

Commercial Investigation (Middle of Funnel):
- "best organic face serum for acne" - 8.9K, Difficulty: 45%
- "organic face serum reviews" - 3.5K, Difficulty: 40%

Transactional (Bottom of Funnel):
- "buy organic face serum" - 2.1K, Difficulty: 50%
- "organic face serum sale" - 1.2K, Difficulty: 35%
- "affordable organic face serum" - 890, Difficulty: 28%
```

**Content Strategy**:
- Create blog posts for informational keywords (build authority)
- Develop comparison pages for commercial keywords (capture researchers)
- Optimize product pages for transactional keywords (convert buyers)
