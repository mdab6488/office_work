### Use Case 2: E-commerce Store Recovery

**Background**: Sarah's handmade jewelry store lost 60% traffic after Google algorithm update

**Problem Diagnosis with SEMrush**:

```
Site Audit Revealed:
- 234 product pages with duplicate descriptions
- Thin content on category pages (<100 words)
- 67 broken links to discontinued products
- No alt text on 890 product images
- Site health score: 42/100
```

**Recovery Plan**:

**Phase 1: Technical Fixes (Week 1-2)**
```
✓ Rewrote 234 product descriptions (unique, 300+ words each)
✓ Expanded category pages to 800+ words with buying guides
✓ Fixed all broken links, set up 301 redirects
✓ Added descriptive alt text to all images
✓ Improved site speed (5.1s → 2.3s)

Site Health Score: 42 → 88
```

**Phase 2: Content Marketing (Month 2-4)**
```
Created Buying Guides:
- "How to Choose the Perfect Engagement Ring"
- "Gemstone Meanings and Properties Guide"
- "Jewelry Care: Complete Maintenance Guide"
- "Matching Jewelry to Your Skin Tone"

All based on SEMrush Topic Research
Average length: 2,000 words
```

**Phase 3: Link Building (Month 3-6)**
```
Strategies:
- Sent free samples to fashion bloggers (earned 23 backlinks)
- Created "Jewelry Trends 2025" infographic (45 backlinks)
- Featured in "Best Handmade Jewelry Shops" roundups (12 backlinks)

Backlinks: 34 → 114 referring domains
```

**Results After 6 Months**:
```
Organic Traffic: Recovered 95% of lost traffic + 30% growth
Product Pages Ranking: 89 → 456 in top 10
Conversion Rate: 1.2% → 2.8%
Monthly Revenue: $12,400 → $34,700
Average Order Value: $67 → $89
```

### Use Case 3: Local Service Business Domination

**Background**: Mike's HVAC company competed with 15 local businesses in Phoenix, Arizona

**SEMrush Local SEO Strategy**:

**Competitor Analysis**:
```
Top Competitor: PhoenixACRepair.com
- Ranking for 89 local keywords
- Getting 2,400 monthly visits
- 67 local citations
- 45 Google reviews

Your Starting Point:
- Ranking for 12 local keywords
- Getting 180 monthly visits
- 15 local citations
- 8 Google reviews
```

**Action Plan**:

**Month 1-2: On-Page Optimization**
```
Created Service Pages:
- AC Repair Phoenix (by neighborhood)
  - "AC Repair Scottsdale"
  - "AC Repair Tempe"  
  - "AC Repair Mesa"
  - etc. (15 neighborhood pages)

Each page included:
- Specific neighborhood information
- Local testimonials
- Service area map
- Emergency contact prominently
- Schema markup for local business
```

**Month 3-4: Citation Building**
```
Built 120 local citations:
- Yelp for Business
- Angie's List
- HomeAdvisor
- Thumbtack
- Better Business Bureau
- Local chamber of commerce
- Industry-specific directories

Ensured NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency everywhere
```

**Month 5-6: Review Generation + Content**
```
Reviews:
- Implemented post-service review request system
- Grew from 8 to 87 Google reviews
- Average rating: 4.8 stars

Content:
- "HVAC Maintenance Tips for Phoenix Climate"
- "How to Reduce AC Costs in Arizona Summer"
- "Signs Your AC Needs Repair in Phoenix"
```

**Results After 6 Months**:
```
Rankings:
- "AC repair Phoenix" - Position 3 (was not ranking)
- "HVAC service Phoenix" - Position 2
- "air conditioning repair Phoenix" - Position 4
- "emergency AC repair Phoenix" - Position 5

Organic Traffic: 180 → 3,400 monthly visits
Phone Calls: +340%
Service Appointments: 23/month → 94/month
Revenue: $28,000/month → $87,000/month
```
