
## Introduction to SEMrush

**SEMrush** is a comprehensive all-in-one digital marketing platform that provides tools for SEO, content marketing, competitor research, PPC, and social media marketing. With over 55+ tools and reports, it's one of the most powerful platforms for digital marketers.

### Key Capabilities
- **Database Size**: 25+ billion keywords across 130+ countries
- **Historical Data**: Access to years of SEO and advertising data
- **Competitive Intelligence**: Analyze competitors' strategies
- **All-in-One Solution**: SEO, PPC, content, social media, and PR tools

### Pricing Tiers (as of 2025)
- **Pro**: $139.95/month - For freelancers and small businesses
- **Guru**: $249.95/month - For growing agencies and mid-size businesses
- **Business**: $499.95/month - For large agencies and enterprises
