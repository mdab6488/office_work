
## Rank Tracker

### What It Does
Monitors your website's keyword rankings over time across different locations and devices.

### Key Features

#### 1. **Setting Up a Project**

**Example**: Tracking a local bakery's rankings

```
Business: Sweet Dreams Bakery (Chicago)
Website: sweetdreamschicago.com

Keywords to Track:
- "bakery chicago" (local focus)
- "best cupcakes chicago"
- "custom birthday cakes chicago"
- "wedding cakes near me"
- "gluten free bakery chicago"

Tracking Settings:
- Location: Chicago, IL, United States
- Device: Desktop & Mobile
- Search Engine: Google
- Frequency: Daily updates
```

#### 2. **Understanding Ranking Reports**

**Real-Life Example**:

**Week 1 - Starting Positions**:
```
Keyword                          | Position | Volume | URL
-----------------------------------------------------------------
bakery chicago                   | 15       | 1,200  | /
best cupcakes chicago           | 23       | 800    | /cupcakes
custom birthday cakes chicago   | 8        | 600    | /birthday-cakes
wedding cakes near me           | 42       | 2,400  | /wedding-cakes
gluten free bakery chicago      | 5        | 400    | /gluten-free
```

**Week 4 - After Optimization**:
```
Keyword                          | Position | Change | Traffic Impact
------------------------------------------------------------------------
bakery chicago                   | 12       | ↑3     | +15 visits/month
best cupcakes chicago           | 18       | ↑5     | +25 visits/month
custom birthday cakes chicago   | 4        | ↑4     | +80 visits/month
wedding cakes near me           | 35       | ↑7     | +10 visits/month
gluten free bakery chicago      | 2        | ↑3     | +120 visits/month
```

**Analysis**:
- "gluten free bakery chicago" moved to position 2 = big win!
- "custom birthday cakes" cracked top 5 = strong traffic increase
- "wedding cakes" still needs work (position 35)

#### 3. **Competitor Tracking**

**Scenario**: Track competitors in the same project

```
Your Bakery vs. Competitors:

Keyword: "custom birthday cakes chicago"

Position Tracking:
1. competitorA.com (Position 1)
2. competitorB.com (Position 2)
3. competitorC.com (Position 3)
4. yoursite.com (Position 4) ← You're here!
5. competitorD.com (Position 5)

Insight:
- Competitor A has DR 35, 15 backlinks to that page
- You have DR 20, 8 backlinks
- Gap: Need 5-7 more quality backlinks to overtake them
```

### Practical Use Case: Seasonal Tracking

**Scenario**: You run a tax preparation service

**Strategy**:
```
November-December (Tax Season Prep):
Keywords start ranking: "tax preparation 2024"

January-April (Peak Season):
Track daily:
- "tax preparation near me" (Position 3 → 2 → 1)
- "free tax filing" (Position 15 → 12 → 10)
- "tax accountant [city]" (Position 8 → 6 → 4)

May-October (Off-Season):
Switch to weekly tracking
Focus on informational content:
- "tax deductions for small business"
- "how to reduce taxable income"
```
