## How to Use This Guide

This comprehensive curriculum is designed to take you from beginner to expert in Modern Display Advertising. The content is structured progressively:

- **Sections 1-5:** Foundational knowledge
- **Sections 6-14:** Platform and technical expertise
- **Sections 15-21:** Specialized knowledge areas
- **Sections 22-24:** Advanced analytics and tools
- **Sections 25-30:** Best practices, career development, and future trends

**Recommended Learning Path:**
1. Start with fundamentals (Sections 1-2)
2. Master core platforms (Sections 6-7)
3. Dive deep into programmatic (Sections 5, 13)
4. Develop measurement expertise (Sections 9-12)
5. Specialize in your area of interest (Sections 15-21)
6. Stay current with emerging trends (Section 23, 30)

Each section can be studied independently, but following the sequential order will provide the most comprehensive understanding.
