#### **LinkedIn Relationship Strategy**

**Real-Life Example:**

**Target:** CMO at major SaaS company who regularly shares marketing insights

```
Month 1: Observation
- Follow and engage with posts
- Study their content themes
- Note what gets most engagement

Month 2: Value Engagement
- Comment with data-backed insights
- Share their posts with thoughtful commentary
- Connect with their network thoughtfully

Example Comment:
"Your point about CAC payback period is critical. We reduced ours 
from 18 to 7 months by segmenting leads by 'product awareness stage' 
instead of traditional demographics. Completely changed our nurture 
sequences. Would love to share the framework if useful."

"Hi [Name], I've been following your insights on B2B marketing and 
consistently learn something new. Would love to connect and continue 
the conversation. [Specific reference to their recent post]."

Month 4: Natural Ask
```
