
### 1. Infographics

#### **What Makes Infographics Linkable?**

**Real-Life Example: The "Perfect Sleep Environment" Infographic**

**Background:**
HealthySleepHub.com created an infographic about optimal sleep conditions.

**Key Elements:**

```
✓ Original Data: Surveyed 10,000 people about sleep environments
✓ Visual Hierarchy: Clear sections (Temperature, Light, Sound, Bedding)
✓ Actionable Insights: "68°F is optimal for 73% of respondents"
✓ Shareable Size: 800px wide, perfect for blog posts
✓ Embed Code: Provided code for easy sharing with attribution
✓ Contrasting Colors: Blue/white theme (sleep-appropriate)
✓ Icons: Simple, recognizable symbols
✓ Sources Cited: All statistics linked to studies
```

**Promotion Strategy:**

```
Step 1: Create dedicated landing page
URL: healthysleephub.com/perfect-sleep-environment-infographic

Step 2: Write accompanying blog post
- Expand on each infographic section
- Add detailed explanations
- Include embed code
- Create social media snippets

Step 3: Outreach Campaign
Target: 100 websites that previously published sleep content

Email Template:
"Hi [Name],

I saw your article on [Sleep Topic] and thought your readers might 
find this useful:

We surveyed 10,000 people about their sleep environments and created 
an infographic with the findings:

[Link to Infographic]

Key finding: 68°F is the optimal sleep temperature for 73% of people, 
but 61% keep their rooms above 72°F (explaining why they sleep poorly).

Free to use with attribution if you think your readers would benefit!

Best,
[Name]"
```

**Results:**
- 47 websites embedded the infographic
- 32 dofollow backlinks
- 3,200 social shares
- Featured in Sleep Foundation article
