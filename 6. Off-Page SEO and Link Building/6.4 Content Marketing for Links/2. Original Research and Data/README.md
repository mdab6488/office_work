#### **How to Conduct Your Own Research (Even with Small Budget)**

**Example: Local Coffee Shop Chain Research**

**Research Question:**
"What makes customers choose one coffee shop over another?"

**Method:**

```
Step 1: Survey Design (Free: Google Forms)
- Target: 1,000 coffee drinkers
- Questions: 15 multiple choice + 3 open-ended
- Time: 5-7 minutes to complete
- Incentive: Enter to win $50 gift card

Step 2: Data Collection
- Share on social media
- Post in coffee Facebook groups
- Reddit r/coffee (with moderator permission)
- Email to existing customers
- Cost: $50 gift card = $50 total

Step 3: Analysis
- Google Sheets for basic statistics
- Free visualization tools (Canva, Datawrapper)
- ChatGPT for help with data interpretation

Step 4: Publication
- Write comprehensive blog post
- Create infographic of key findings
- Submit press release to local media
```

**Results:**

```
Findings Published: "1,000 Coffee Drinkers Reveal What Really Matters"

Key Insights:
- 67% prioritize coffee quality over ambiance
- 43% will drive an extra 5 miles for better coffee
- Wi-Fi speed matters more than free Wi-Fi (48% vs 31%)
- 78% check Instagram before visiting new coffee shop

Links Earned:
- Local food bloggers (15 links)
- Regional newspapers (3 links)
- Coffee industry blogs (8 links)
- Total: 26 backlinks from $50 investment
```
