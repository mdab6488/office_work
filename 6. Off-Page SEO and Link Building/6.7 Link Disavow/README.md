## 6.7 Link Disavow

### Overview
Link disavowing is the process of telling Google to ignore certain backlinks to your website. This is crucial when you have toxic or spammy backlinks that could harm your search rankings.

---

## Summary & Best Practices

### Key Takeaways

**Social Media Signals:**
- Not a direct ranking factor but drives indirect SEO benefits
- Focus on creating shareable content
- Optimize Open Graph tags for better social sharing
- Track brand mentions and convert unlinked mentions to links

**Brand Building:**
- Strong brands rank better and get more backlinks naturally
- Increase brand searches through consistent marketing
- Build online reputation proactively
- Use influencer partnerships for authority and reach

**Link Disavow:**
- Last resort tool for toxic backlinks
- Conduct regular link audits (quarterly)
- Focus on manual removal first
- Protect against negative SEO with monitoring

### Final Recommendations

```markdown
Monthly SEO Maintenance Checklist:

Social & Brand:
□ Share 2-3 pieces of content on social media
□ Monitor brand mentions (Google Alerts)
□ Respond to all reviews within 24 hours
□ Track brand search volume

Link Management:
□ Review new backlinks in Ahrefs
□ Check for toxic links
□ Update disavow file if needed
□ Monitor for negative SEO

Reporting:
□ Track key metrics month-over-month
□ Document wins and issues
□ Adjust strategy based on data
```
