
### 6.6.1 Brand Authority

#### What Is Brand Authority?

Brand authority is the level of trust, credibility, and influence your brand has in your industry. Search engines favor brands with strong authority because they provide better user experiences.

#### Google's Brand Preference Signals

**1. Branded Search Volume**
- People searching specifically for your brand name
- "YourBrand + keyword" search patterns
- Indicates trust and loyalty

**2. Direct Traffic**
- Users typing your URL directly
- Returning visitors
- Shows brand recall

**3. Engagement Metrics**
- Low bounce rates
- High time on site
- Multiple page visits

**4. Social Proof**
- Reviews and ratings
- Social media following
- Press coverage

#### Building Brand Authority: Step-by-Step

**Real-Life Example: Shopify's Brand Authority Journey**

**Phase 1: Establish Expertise (Months 1-6)**
```
What Shopify Did:
✓ Published comprehensive e-commerce guides
✓ Created free business name generator tool
✓ Launched educational YouTube channel
✓ Hosted free webinars on starting online stores

Result:
- Positioned as e-commerce experts
- 100,000+ organic visitors monthly
- Built email list of 50,000+
```

**Phase 2: Create Unique Value (Months 6-18)**
```
What Shopify Did:
✓ Developed free themes for store owners
✓ Launched Shopify Academy (free courses)
✓ Published annual commerce trends report
✓ Created Burst (free stock photos)

Result:
- Became go-to resource for entrepreneurs
- Featured in TechCrunch, Forbes, Wired
- 1M+ organic visitors monthly
```

**Phase 3: Build Community (Months 18-36)**
```
What Shopify Did:
✓ Launched community forums
✓ Created Facebook group (500K+ members)
✓ Started podcast series
✓ Hosted annual partner conference

Result:
- Organic brand mentions skyrocketed
- 50,000+ backlinks from community content
- "Shopify" became synonymous with e-commerce
```

**Phase 4: Industry Leadership (Year 3+)**
```
What Shopify Did:
✓ Acquired other companies in the space
✓ Sponsored major e-commerce events
✓ CEO became thought leader on Twitter
✓ Published economic impact studies

Result:
- Ranks #1 for 100,000+ keywords
- 80% of e-commerce content mentions Shopify
- Valuation: $100+ billion
```
