
## Email Marketing

### What It Is
Email marketing involves distributing content directly to subscribers' inboxes. It's one of the highest ROI channels (averaging $36-42 for every $1 spent).

### Types of Email Content Distribution

#### 1. **Newsletter Campaigns**

**Real-Life Example: Morning Brew**
- Daily business news in conversational tone
- Consistent 6 AM send time
- Witty writing style that's easy to scan
- Result: 4+ million subscribers, 40%+ open rate

**Structure**:
```
Subject: ‚òï [Curiosity-driven headline]

```

**Best Practices**:
- Send consistently (same day, same time)
- Segment by interests/behavior
- A/B test subject lines
- Optimal send times: Tuesday-Thursday, 10 AM or 2 PM

#### 3. **Automated Drip Campaigns**

**Real-Life Example: Grammarly**
- Welcome series for new users
- Educational content about features
- Usage tips based on behavior
- Result: High activation and retention rates

**Welcome Series Example**:
```
Day 1: Welcome! Here's how to get started
Day 3: Your first win - [Quick value demonstration]
Day 7: Advanced tips for power users
Day 14: Join our community + invite friends
Day 30: We'd love your feedback
```

#### 4. **Behavioral Trigger Emails**

**Real-Life Example: Amazon**
- Cart abandonment reminders
- "You might also like" recommendations
- Re-engagement campaigns
- Result: 3x higher conversion than regular emails

**Triggers**:
- Downloaded a lead magnet ‚Üí Send related content
- Read blog post ‚Üí Send case study on topic
- Attended webinar ‚Üí Send recording + resources
- Inactive for 60 days ‚Üí Win-back campaign

### Email List Segmentation Strategies

**By Demographics**:
- Industry (SaaS, E-commerce, Healthcare)
- Company size (Startup, SMB, Enterprise)
- Job role (Marketer, Developer, Executive)

**By Behavior**:
- Engagement level (Active, Moderate, Cold)
- Content preferences (Video, Articles, Podcasts)
- Purchase history or stage in funnel

**Real-Life Example: Airbnb**
- Segments by travel preferences (beach, city, adventure)
- Location-based offers
- Past booking behavior
- Result: Highly personalized recommendations, higher booking rates

### Email Design Best Practices

**Mobile Optimization** (60%+ of emails opened on mobile):
- Single column layout
- Large, tappable buttons (44√ó44 pixels minimum)
- Font size 14px+ for body text
- Compress images for fast loading

**Accessibility**:
- Alt text for images
- High contrast text/background
- Semantic HTML structure
- Avoid relying solely on color to convey information

### Key Metrics
- **Open Rate**: 15-25% is average (varies by industry)
- **Click-Through Rate**: 2-5% is typical
- **Conversion Rate**: Depends on goal (1-5% average)
- **Unsubscribe Rate**: Under 0.5% is healthy
