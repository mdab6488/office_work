## 9.4 International Link Building

## Key Takeaways

### International Content
1. **Localize, don't just translate** - Adapt to culture, not just language
2. **Use native currency and units** - Meet users' expectations
3. **Offer local payment methods** - Essential for conversion
4. **Adapt content seasonally** - Different hemispheres, different seasons

### International Link Building
1. **Focus on country-specific domains** - .de for Germany, .fr for France, etc.
2. **Build quality over quantity** - 10 relevant local links > 100 random links
3. **Use local citation sources** - Every country has its own directories
4. **Maintain NAP consistency** - Critical for local SEO success
