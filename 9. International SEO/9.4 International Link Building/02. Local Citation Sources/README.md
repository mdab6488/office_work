#### Citation Building Strategy

**Step-by-Step Process:**

**1. Audit Current Citations**

```
Tool: Whitespark Local Citation Finder, Moz Local, or BrightLocal

Check:
- Where are you currently listed?
- Is your NAP consistent everywhere?
- Are there duplicate listings?
- Are there incorrect listings?
```

**2. Claim and Verify Existing Listings**

**Real Example Issue:**

**3. Build Citations Systematically**

**Priority Order:**

```
Tier 1 - Must Have (Do First):
1. Google Business Profile
2. Facebook Business
3. Apple Maps
4. Bing Places
5. National Yellow Pages (country-specific)

Tier 2 - High Value:
6. Industry-specific directories (top 10 in your field)
7. Local chambers of commerce
8. Better Business Bureau (or local equivalent)
9. Local news websites (business directories)

Tier 3 - Nice to Have:
10. Secondary directories
11. Neighborhood/city-specific sites
12. Association memberships
```

#### NAP Consistency is CRITICAL

**The Golden Rule:**

```
Your NAP (Name, Address, Phone) must be EXACTLY the same
across ALL platforms, down to:
- Punctuation
- Abbreviations
- Spacing
- Phone format
```
