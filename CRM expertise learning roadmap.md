## Key Skills to Develop

1. **Technical Skills**: CRM platforms, integrations, customization, data management
2. **Business Skills**: Strategy, ROI analysis, change management, project management
3. **Analytical Skills**: Data analysis, reporting, predictive modeling, optimization
4. **Soft Skills**: Communication, training, stakeholder management, problem-solving
