
### Question-Based Content

Structure content around actual questions people ask.

**Finding Question Keywords:**

#### Method 1: People Also Ask

```
1. Google your main keyword
2. Look at "People Also Ask" section
3. Click to expand questions
4. Note the related questions that appear
5. Create content answering these questions
```

**Example Research for "Home Security Systems":**

```markdown
People Also Ask:
- How much does a home security system cost?
- Do security systems really deter burglars?
- Can I install a security system myself?
- Are wireless security systems better than wired?
- What's the difference between monitored and unmonitored systems?
- How long do security camera batteries last?
```

**Content Strategy:**

```markdown
Main Article: "Home Security Systems: Complete 2024 Guide"

H2: How Much Does a Home Security System Cost?
[40-60 word direct answer]
[Detailed breakdown]

H2: Do Security Systems Really Deter Burglars?
[40-60 word direct answer]
[Statistics and studies]

H2: Can I Install a Security System Myself?
[40-60 word direct answer]
[DIY vs professional comparison]

[Continue for each question...]
```

#### Method 2: AnswerThePublic

**Example Query: "Coffee"**

```
Who questions:
- Who invented coffee?
- Who drinks the most coffee?
- Who sells the best coffee beans?

What questions:
- What coffee has the most caffeine?
- What coffee does Starbucks use?
- What's the difference between arabica and robusta?

Where questions:
- Where is coffee grown?
- Where to buy fresh coffee beans near me?
- Where does Starbucks get their coffee?

When questions:
- When is the best time to drink coffee?
- When does coffee expire?
- When was coffee discovered?

Why questions:
- Why does coffee make me sleepy?
- Why is coffee good for you?
- Why does coffee stain teeth?

How questions:
- How to make cold brew coffee?
- How much coffee is too much?
- How to store coffee beans?
```

**Content Creation:**

```markdown
# Ultimate Coffee Guide: Everything You Need to Know

## Table of Contents
- What Coffee Has the Most Caffeine?
- How to Make Cold Brew Coffee at Home
- Why Does Coffee Make Some People Sleepy?
- When is the Best Time to Drink Coffee?
- How to Store Coffee Beans for Maximum Freshness

[Create individual sections for each question]
```
