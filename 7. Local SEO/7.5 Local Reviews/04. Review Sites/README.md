### **Yelp**

**Importance: ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê (Critical for restaurants, home services, healthcare)**

**Why It Matters:**
- 178 million monthly users (U.S.)
- Users have high purchase intent
- Strong local SEO value (DA 95)
- Appears in Google search results
- Particularly strong in California, New York, Chicago

**Yelp Algorithm:**
- Filters reviews from accounts with few reviews
- Favors reviews from "Yelp Elite" members
- Doesn't allow incentivized reviews (strictly enforced)
- Penalizes businesses for asking for reviews directly

**Best Practices:**
- **DO:**
  - Claim and complete business profile
  - Respond to all reviews (positive and negative)
  - Upload high-quality photos regularly
  - Keep business information updated
  - Encourage customers to use Yelp generally
  
- **DON'T:**
  - Ask for reviews directly
  - Incentivize reviews
  - Review yourself or ask family
  - Attempt to manipulate reviews

**Yelp Advertising:**
- Costs $200-$1,000+/month
- Removes competitor ads from your page
- Gives prominent placement
- Worth it for competitive industries (restaurants, salons)

**Best For:**
- Restaurants and bars
- Beauty salons and spas
- Home services (plumbers, electricians)
- Auto repair
- Healthcare providers
- Active nightlife/entertainment

**Dealing with Platform-Specific Issues:**

### **Yelp's Filter:**
**Problem:** Yelp filters 20-25% of reviews

**What Gets Filtered:**
- Reviews from accounts with no other reviews
- First review on a new account
- Reviews from accounts with few friends/photos
- Suspicious patterns

**What You Can Do:**
- Accept it (filtered reviews still count partially)
- Encourage active Yelp users to review
- NEVER ask Yelp to unfilter
- Focus on quality over quantity

### **Google Review Removal:**
**Problem:** Google removes reviews that violate policy

**Removable Reviews:**
- Spam or fake
- Off-topic
- Conflict of interest
- Illegal content
- Sexually explicit
- Personal attacks

**How to Request Removal:**
- Flag through Google Business Profile
- Provide evidence if available
- Be patient (can take weeks)

### **Facebook Review Removal:**
**Problem:** Can't remove negative reviews

**What You Can Do:**
- Respond professionally
- Use private response feature
- Report if violates Facebook policies
- Overwhelm with positive reviews

**Advanced: Review Funnel Strategy:**

**Internal Feedback First:**
1. Customer completes service
2. Send satisfaction survey privately
3. If positive (4-5 stars) ‚Üí Direct to public review
4. If negative (1-3 stars) ‚Üí Internal complaint form

**Example Flow:**
```
Email 1 (same day):
"How did we do? [Rate 1-5 stars]"

If 5 stars:
"We're thrilled! Would you share on Google? [link]"

If 1-3 stars:
"We're sorry we missed the mark. Please tell us what happened 
so we can make it right: [link to private form]"
```

**Benefits:**
- Reduces public negative reviews
- Gives chance to fix issues before they go public
- Increases positive review rate

**Important:** Don't ONLY ask happy customers for public reviews and unhappy customers for private feedback - that can violate platform policies. Must be transparent about process.
