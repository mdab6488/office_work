## 7.7 Voice Search and Local

Voice search has fundamentally changed how people find local businesses. By 2024, over 50% of all searches are voice-based, and voice searches have a 3x higher local intent than text searches.

### Understanding Voice Search Behavior

#### Text Search vs. Voice Search


**Key Differences:**

| Aspect | Text Search | Voice Search |
|--------|-------------|--------------|
| **Length** | 2-3 words | 7-10 words |
| **Tone** | Keyword-focused | Conversational |
| **Intent** | Research & Compare | Immediate Action |
| **Format** | Keywords | Questions |
| **Context** | Often missing | Usually included |
