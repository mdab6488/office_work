
### 7.1.4 GBP Posts

#### What Are GBP Posts?

Posts appear directly in your Google Business Profile and Google Maps listing. They're mini-ads that disappear after 7 days (except events).

#### Types of Posts

**1. What's New Posts**

**Real Example - "Mountain View Dental":**

```
Title: New Patient Special - $99 Comprehensive Exam!

Body: Welcome to Mountain View Dental! We're offering new patients 
a comprehensive dental exam, cleaning, and X-rays for just $99 
(regular $350 value). Our experienced team uses the latest technology 
for comfortable, efficient care. Book your appointment today!

Call to Action Button: "Book Now"
Link: www.mountainviewdental.com/new-patient-special
Image: Smiling patient with dentist
```

**2. Event Posts**

**Real Example - "Riverside Books & Coffee":**

```
Title: Meet Local Author Sarah Chen!

Event Date: November 15, 2025
Time: 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Body: Join us for an afternoon with bestselling author Sarah Chen 
as she discusses her new mystery novel "Echoes in the Fog." Book 
signing, Q&A, and complimentary coffee included. Seats limited!

Call to Action: "Learn More"
Link: www.riversidebooks.com/events/sarah-chen
Image: Book cover + author photo
```

**Why Event Posts Are Special:**
- They don't expire after 7 days
- Show up until the event date passes
- Appear in event-specific searches

**3. Offer Posts**

**Real Example - "Apex Auto Repair":**

```
Title: 20% OFF Brake Service This Month

Offer: 20% Discount
Valid: November 1-30, 2025

Body: Safety first! Get 20% off complete brake service including 
pads, rotors, and inspection. Our ASE-certified technicians use 
premium parts with a 2-year warranty. Don't wait until it's too late!

Terms: Cannot be combined with other offers. Present coupon at 
time of service.

Call to Action: "Redeem Offer"
Coupon Code: BRAKE20
Image: Mechanic working on brakes
```

**4. Product Posts**

**Real Example - "Artisan Furniture Co.":**

```
Title: Handcrafted Walnut Dining Table

Price: $2,400
Category: Furniture

Body: Custom-made from sustainably sourced American Black Walnut. 
Seats 6-8 people comfortably. Features live edge design with 
steel hairpin legs. Each piece is unique. Custom sizes available.

Call to Action: "Buy"
Link: www.artisanfurnitureco.com/walnut-dining-table
Images: 4 photos (different angles)
```

#### Posting Strategy and Schedule

**Optimal Posting Frequency:**
- Minimum: 1 post per week
- Ideal: 2-3 posts per week
- Maximum: 1 post per day

**Real Weekly Schedule - "Sunrise Fitness Center":**

```
MONDAY: Motivational post/class schedule
"New week, new goals! Join us for High-Intensity Interval Training 
at 6 AM and 6 PM. First class free for new members!"

WEDNESDAY: Member spotlight/testimonial
"Member Spotlight: Meet Jennifer who lost 40 lbs this year! 
'The supportive community at Sunrise made all the difference.'"

FRIDAY: Weekend offer/event announcement
"Weekend Warriors Special! Bring a friend for FREE this Saturday. 
Try our new equipment and meet our trainers. 9 AM - 12 PM."
```

#### Post Performance Metrics

**Real Results - "Lakeside Restaurant":**

Post tracking over 3 months:

| Post Type | Weekly Posts | Views | Clicks | Conversion |
|-----------|--------------|-------|--------|------------|
| Menu Specials | 2 | 1,450 | 87 | 12 reservations |
| Events | 1 | 2,100 | 156 | 34 reservations |
| General Updates | 2 | 890 | 43 | 6 inquiries |

**Key Insight:** Event posts generated 300% more engagement than general updates.

#### Post Optimization Tips

**Image Requirements:**
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Size: Minimum 400x300px, Recommended 750x750px
- Aspect Ratio: 4:3 for best display
- File Size: Under 5MB

**Writing Tips:**

**Before (Weak Post):**
```
Title: Sale

We have a sale going on. Come check us out for great deals on all items.
```

**After (Strong Post):**
```
Title: 40% Off Fall Collection - This Week Only!

Our entire fall collection is now 40% off! From cozy sweaters to 
stylish jackets, refresh your wardrobe for less. Premium brands 
including North Face, Patagonia, and Columbia. Shop in-store or 
online with code FALL40.

Call to Action: "Shop Now"
```

**What Changed:**
- Specific discount percentage
- Clear timeframe
- Specific items mentioned
- Brand names added
- Code provided for tracking
