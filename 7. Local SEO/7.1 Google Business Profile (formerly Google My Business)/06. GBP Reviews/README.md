

### 7.1.6 GBP Reviews

#### Why Reviews Matter

**Statistics:**
- 93% of consumers read online reviews before making a purchase
- Businesses with 3.5+ star rating see 3x more clicks than those under 3.5
- Reviews account for approximately 15% of local ranking factors

**Real Impact - "Maple Street Bakery":**
```
Before Review Campaign (2.8 stars, 12 reviews):
- Monthly calls: 45
- Direction requests: 67
- Website visits: 156

After Review Campaign (4.7 stars, 147 reviews):
- Monthly calls: 178 (+296%)
- Direction requests: 312 (+366%)
- Website visits: 543 (+248%)
```

#### What NOT to Do (Violations)

**‚ùå ILLEGAL/AGAINST POLICY:**

1. **Incentivizing Reviews**
```
"Leave a 5-star review and get 10% off your next visit!"
```

2. **Selective Asking**
```
"If you're happy, leave a Google review. If not, just email us."
```

3. **Review Gating** (Filtering before asking)
```
Survey: "Rate your experience 1-5"
[If 4-5] ‚Üí Ask for Google review
[If 1-3] ‚Üí Don't ask for review
```

4. **Buying Reviews**
```
"We'll pay you $10 for a 5-star review"
```

5. **Fake Reviews**
```
Writing reviews from fake accounts or asking employees/family 
to pose as customers
```

**Penalties:** Google may suspend your listing permanently.

#### Responding to Reviews

**Response Rate Impact:**
- Businesses that respond to reviews see 12% higher ratings on average
- Response rate correlates with better rankings

**Response Time Best Practice:** Within 48 hours (ideally 24 hours)

#### Responding to Negative Reviews

**The Golden Rule:** Stay professional, empathetic, and solution-focused.

**Real Example 1 - Legitimate Complaint:**

```
Customer Review:
"Very disappointed. Waited over an hour past my appointment time 
with no explanation. The food was good but the service ruined the 
experience. Won't be returning."
- Lisa Chen

‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÜ‚òÜ‚òÜ

GOOD RESPONSE:
"Lisa, we sincerely apologize for your experience. An hour wait 
with no communication is completely unacceptable, and we take 
full responsibility. This doesn't reflect the service we strive 
to provide. I'd like to understand what happened and make this 
right. Please contact me directly at owner@harbourrestaurant.com 
or (555) 234-5678. We've implemented a new system to prevent this, 
and I'd welcome the chance to restore your faith in us."
- David Park, Owner
Harbour Restaurant

---

BAD RESPONSE (Defensive):
"We were very busy that night. You should have called ahead. The 
food was good so I don't understand the 2-star rating."

---

BAD RESPONSE (Dismissive):
"Sorry for any inconvenience."
```

**What Makes It Good:**
- Immediate, sincere apology
- Takes responsibility (no excuses)
- Acknowledges specific issue
- Offers private resolution channel
- Shows improvement action
- Remains professional
- Owner involvement shows it matters

#### Review Response Template Library

**Template for 5-Star Reviews:**
```
"Thank you so much, [Name]! [Specific acknowledgment of what they 
mentioned]. We're thrilled that [specific aspect] met your 
expectations. [Team member name if mentioned] will be so happy to 
hear this! We appreciate your business and look forward to serving 
you again soon."
```

**Template for 3-4 Star Reviews:**
```
"Thanks for your feedback, [Name]! We're glad [positive aspect they 
mentioned] worked well. Regarding [negative aspect], [explanation 
and/or what you're doing to improve]. We'd love another chance to 
provide a 5-star experience. Please reach out if you'd like to 
discuss further."
```

**Template for 1-2 Star Reviews:**
```
"[Name], we sincerely apologize for [specific issue]. This isn't 
the experience we want for our customers. [Brief explanation without 
excuses]. Please contact us at [email/phone] so we can make this 
right. We've [action taken to prevent future issues]."
```
