

### 7.1.7 GBP Insights

#### Understanding GBP Insights

Insights is your analytics dashboard showing how customers find and interact with your listing.

**Access:** Google Business Profile → Performance (formerly "Insights")

#### Key Metrics Explained

**1. Search Queries**

Shows what people searched to find your business.

**Real Example - "Mountain Coffee Roasters":**

```
TOP SEARCHES (Last 30 Days):

Direct Searches (people searching for your business specifically):
1. "mountain coffee roasters" - 234 searches
2. "mountain coffee" - 89 searches
3. "mountain coffee roasters seattle" - 67 searches
4. "mountain coffee capitol hill" - 45 searches

Discovery Searches (people searching for your type of business):
1. "coffee shop near me" - 456 impressions, 23 clicks
2. "best coffee seattle" - 234 impressions, 12 clicks
3. "coffee roaster capitol hill" - 189 impressions, 19 clicks
4. "specialty coffee near me" - 156 impressions, 8 clicks
5. "pour over coffee seattle" - 112 impressions, 7 clicks
```

**Insights from This Data:**
- Strong brand recognition (234 direct searches)
- "near me" searches converting well (5% click rate)
- "pour over coffee" is a lower-volume but relevant term to focus content on

**Action Items:**
- Create GBP post about pour-over coffee methods
- Add "pour over coffee" to business description
- Train staff to mention in reviews

- Monday morning peak → People calling after weekend car issues
- 3m 42s duration → Enough time for conversation, good engagement
- Action: Ensure adequate Monday morning phone coverage

From Messages:

- 4-hour response time → Good, but could improve to 2 hours
- Action: Set up auto-reply for off-hours

#### Using Insights for Strategy

**Real Case Study - "Harbor Pet Grooming":**

**Month 1 - Baseline Insights:**
```
Search Views: 1,200
Website Clicks: 67 (5.6% CTR)
Phone Calls: 34 (2.8% conversion)
Direction Requests: 45 (3.8% conversion)
Total Actions: 146 (12.2% action rate)
```

**Analysis:**
- Low action rate compared to industry average (15-20%)
- Website clicks okay, but calls and directions low
- Need to make taking action easier

**Changes Made:**
1. Updated business hours (were incorrect)
2. Added phone number to description
3. Created "Book Online" button
4. Posted 3x per week (was 1x)
5. Added 15 new high-quality photos
6. Started review collection campaign
7. Answered all Q&A questions proactively

**Month 3 - Results:**
```
Search Views: 2,340 (+95%)
Website Clicks: 198 (8.5% CTR) (+296%)
Phone Calls: 87 (3.7% conversion) (+256%)
Direction Requests: 112 (4.8% conversion) (+249%)
Message Inquiries: 23 (new feature)
Total Actions: 420 (18% action rate) (+288%)

ROI:
New customers from GBP: 89
Average customer value: $85
Revenue from optimization: $7,565/month
Time invested: 5 hours/month
ROI: 1,513% (excluding paid ads)
```
