
### 7.1.5 GBP Q&A

#### What is GBP Q&A?

The Questions & Answers section allows customers to ask questions directly on your Google Business Profile. Anyone can answer - including competitors!

**Critical Importance:** 35% of potential customers read Q&A before visiting or calling.

#### Proactive Q&A Strategy

**Don't wait for questions - seed your own!**

**Real Example - "Harbor Veterinary Clinic":**

**Self-Posted Q&A:**

```
Q: Do you offer emergency services?
A: Yes! Harbor Veterinary Clinic provides emergency services during 
regular business hours (Mon-Fri 8AM-7PM, Sat 9AM-4PM). For 
after-hours emergencies, we partner with BluePearl Emergency Pet 
Hospital. Call us at (555) 123-4567 or visit their 24/7 facility 
at 456 Oak Street.

Posted by: Harbor Veterinary Clinic

---

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, 
Discover), debit cards, cash, and checks. We also work with 
CareCredit for payment plans. Pet insurance claims can be 
submitted directly through our office.

Posted by: Harbor Veterinary Clinic

---

Q: Do I need an appointment or do you accept walk-ins?
A: We operate primarily by appointment to minimize wait times 
and provide the best care. However, we reserve spots daily for 
urgent walk-in cases. Call ahead if possible, and we'll do our 
best to accommodate you. Emergency cases are seen immediately.

Posted by: Harbor Veterinary Clinic

---

Q: Are you accepting new patients?
A: Absolutely! We're welcoming new patients and their pet parents. 
New clients receive a complimentary wellness exam with their first 
visit. Book online at www.harborvet.com or call (555) 123-4567. 
We're excited to meet you and your furry family!

Posted by: Harbor Veterinary Clinic
```

#### Essential Questions to Answer Proactively

**For Restaurants:**
- Do you have vegetarian/vegan options?
- Do you take reservations?
- Is there parking available?
- Do you offer takeout/delivery?
- Are you kid-friendly?
- What are your most popular dishes?
- Do you have a private room for events?

**For Service Businesses:**
- Do you offer free estimates?
- What areas do you serve?
- Are you licensed and insured?
- What are your emergency hours?
- What payment methods do you accept?
- How quickly can you respond?
- Do you offer warranties/guarantees?

**For Retail:**
- Do you have [specific product]?
- Can you order items not in stock?
- What is your return policy?
- Do you offer gift wrapping?
- Do you price match?
- Is there parking?
- Are dogs allowed?

#### Managing Customer Questions

**Real Example - Customer Asked Question:**

```
Q: "How much does a typical roof replacement cost?"
Posted by: Michael T.

GOOD ANSWER (by business owner):
A: Great question, Michael! Roof replacement costs vary based on 
size, materials, and complexity. For a typical 2,000 sq ft home:
- Asphalt shingles: $8,000-$12,000
- Architectural shingles: $12,000-$16,000
- Metal roofing: $15,000-$25,000
- Tile roofing: $20,000-$35,000

We offer FREE detailed estimates including inspection, materials 
breakdown, and timeline. Call us at (555) ROOF-NOW or book online. 
We're here to help!
- Elite Roofing Solutions

---

BAD ANSWER:
A: It depends. Call us for a quote.
```

**Why the Good Answer Works:**
- Acknowledges the asker by name
- Provides specific, helpful information
- Shows expertise with detailed ranges
- Includes multiple options
- Clear call-to-action
- Professional signature

#### Q&A Monitoring and Response Time

**Best Practices:**
- Check Q&A daily
- Respond within 24 hours (ideally within 4 hours)
- Set up notifications via Google Business Profile app

**Real Process - "Summit Physical Therapy":**

```
DAILY ROUTINE:
9:00 AM - Check GBP Q&A section
- New questions: Flag for immediate response
- Spam/inappropriate: Report and ignore
- Competitor answers: Add business owner response if needed

Throughout Day:
- Mobile app notifications enabled
- Team member designated for monitoring
- Responses drafted and approved by manager

End of Day:
- Quick check for any late questions
- Review answered questions for completeness
```

#### Dealing with Competitor or Spam Answers

**Scenario:** A competitor posted misleading information.

**Real Example:**

```
Q: "Does Clean Pro Carpet Cleaning use eco-friendly products?"

Competitor Answer: "I heard they use harsh chemicals. Try GreenClean 
Services instead - we're 100% natural!"

YOUR RESPONSE:
Yes! Clean Pro exclusively uses EPA Safer Choice certified products 
that are safe for children, pets, and the environment. Our green 
cleaning solutions are plant-based and biodegradable. We've been 
certified by the Green Seal program since 2019. See our full product 
list and certifications at www.cleanprocarpet.com/eco-friendly. 
We're happy to discuss our process - call us at (555) 234-5678!
- Clean Pro Carpet Cleaning, Owner
```

**Also:** Report the competitor's answer as inappropriate/spam.
