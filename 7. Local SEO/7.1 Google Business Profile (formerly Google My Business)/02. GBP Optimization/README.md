
### 7.1.2 GBP Optimization

#### Complete Profile Checklist

**Essential Elements:**

**1. Business Name**
- Use your actual, legal business name
- Don't add keywords or location

**Wrong:** 
```
Smith & Associates Law Firm | Best Lawyers in Chicago | Personal Injury
```

**Right:**
```
Smith & Associates Law Firm
```

**2. Business Description (750 characters)**

**Real Example - "Green Leaf Landscaping":**
```
Green Leaf Landscaping has been transforming residential and commercial 
properties in Denver for over 15 years. We specialize in sustainable 
landscaping, xeriscaping, native plant installation, and year-round 
maintenance. Our certified team creates beautiful outdoor spaces that 
conserve water and support local ecosystems. From design to installation 
to ongoing care, we bring your landscape vision to life. Family-owned 
and operated, we pride ourselves on quality craftsmanship and 
exceptional customer service. Licensed and insured. Free consultations 
available.
```

**Key Elements in This Description:**
- What they do (landscaping services)
- Experience (15 years)
- Specializations (xeriscaping, native plants)
- Service area (Denver)
- Unique selling points (sustainable, family-owned)
- Call to action (free consultations)

**3. Business Hours**

Be accurate and update for holidays!

**Real Example - "Brew Coffee House":**
```
Monday:       6:00 AM – 8:00 PM
Tuesday:      6:00 AM – 8:00 PM
Wednesday:    6:00 AM – 8:00 PM
Thursday:     6:00 AM – 8:00 PM
Friday:       6:00 AM – 9:00 PM
Saturday:     7:00 AM – 9:00 PM
Sunday:       7:00 AM – 7:00 PM

Special Hours:
Christmas Day: Closed
New Year's Day: 9:00 AM – 2:00 PM
Thanksgiving: Closed
```

**Pro Tip:** Update "Special Hours" 2 weeks before holidays to prevent frustrated customers.

**6. Photos and Videos**

Google recommends:
- Upload photos every 7 days
- Minimum 3 photos
- High quality (720px minimum)

**Photo Strategy - "Coastal Dental Care":**

| Photo Type | Quantity | Examples |
|------------|----------|----------|
| Exterior | 3-5 | Building front, parking lot, signage |
| Interior | 8-12 | Reception area, treatment rooms, waiting area |
| Team | 5-8 | Individual dentist photos, team photos |
| Work/Products | 15-20 | Before/after (with consent), equipment |
| Additional | 10+ | Patient testimonial images, community events |

**Real Result:** After adding 30 high-quality photos, a dental practice saw:
- 42% increase in website clicks
- 29% increase in direction requests
- 56% increase in phone calls

List specific offerings with prices when possible.
