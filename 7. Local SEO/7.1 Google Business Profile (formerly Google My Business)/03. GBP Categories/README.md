
### 7.1.3 GBP Categories

#### Understanding Categories

Categories help Google understand your business and show your listing for relevant searches.

**Category Structure:**
- **Primary Category:** Most important - defines your core business
- **Additional Categories:** Up to 9 more - shows the range of services

#### Choosing the Right Categories

**Real Example 1 - "Bella Vista Italian Restaurant":**

```
Primary Category: Italian Restaurant

Additional Categories:
- Pizza Restaurant
- Wine Bar
- Bar & Grill
- Family Restaurant
- Caterer
```

**Why These Categories:**
- Primary = Core business focus
- Pizza Restaurant = Popular specific service
- Wine Bar = Differentiator from competitors
- Bar & Grill = Captures broader searches
- Family Restaurant = Targets family diners
- Caterer = Additional revenue stream

**Keyword Triggers:**
- "Italian restaurant near me" → Primary category
- "pizza delivery Brooklyn" → Pizza Restaurant category
- "family dinner spot" → Family Restaurant category

**Real Example 2 - "TechFix Computer Repair":**

```
Primary Category: Computer Repair Service

Additional Categories:
- Mobile Phone Repair Service
- Computer Store
- Electronics Repair Shop
- Data Recovery Service
- Computer Support and Services
```

**Result:** After adding specific categories, they ranked for 145 additional keywords and saw 34% more calls.

#### Category Best Practices

**✅ DO:**
- Choose the most specific category available
- Add all relevant categories
- Review categories quarterly (Google adds new ones)

**❌ DON'T:**
- Choose vague categories like "Business"
- Select unrelated categories for visibility
- Use only one category when multiple apply
