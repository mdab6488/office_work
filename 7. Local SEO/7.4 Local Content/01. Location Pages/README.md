
### üìç Location Pages

**What They Are:**
Dedicated pages for each location your business serves or operates in, optimized for local search terms.

**Why They Matter:**
- Target "near me" and location-specific searches
- Improve local pack rankings
- Provide specific information for each area
- Support multi-location SEO strategies

**Real-Life Examples:**

**Example 1: Pest Control Company - Multiple Service Areas**

**Structure:**
```
Main site: PestProServices.com
Location pages:
- /phoenix-pest-control/
- /scottsdale-pest-control/
- /tempe-pest-control/
- /mesa-pest-control/
```

**Phoenix Page Content:**
```
URL: pestproservices.com/phoenix-pest-control/

H1: Phoenix Pest Control Services | PestPro

Content includes:
- Introduction mentioning Phoenix-specific pests (scorpions, bark scorpions)
- Service area map showing Phoenix neighborhoods
- Local statistics: "Phoenix homes face 3x more scorpion issues than other AZ cities"
- Customer testimonials from Phoenix residents (with neighborhood mentioned)
- Photos of actual Phoenix properties serviced
- Local landmarks mentioned: "Serving areas near Desert Botanical Garden, South Mountain"
- Phoenix-specific service hours and contact
- Schema markup with Phoenix geo-coordinates
```

**Results:**
- Ranked #1 for "Phoenix pest control"
- Ranked in local pack for "pest control near me" in Phoenix
- 150% increase in Phoenix-area leads
