**Implementation Strategy:**

**Step 1: Choose Authentic Involvement**

Ask yourself:
- What causes genuinely matter to me?
- What skills can I uniquely offer?
- Where can I make real impact?
- What aligns with my business values?

**Don't volunteer JUST for links** - people can tell, and it often backfires.

**Step 2: Start Small**

Begin with:
- One commitment you can sustain long-term
- 2-3 hours per month maximum initially
- Something close to your business/expertise
- Organization you already have connection to

**Step 3: Document (Without Bragging)**

Appropriate documentation:
- Photos of your involvement (posted naturally)
- "Behind the scenes" of volunteer work
- Celebrating the org's accomplishments (not your contribution)
- Sharing org's events and needs

Inappropriate:
- "Look how generous we are" posts
- Using beneficiaries as marketing props
- Making it about you instead of the cause

**Step 4: Let Others Tell Your Story**

Better than self-promotion:
- Organizations naturally mention supporters
- Beneficiaries share gratitude organically
- Media covers your involvement (if newsworthy)
- Community recognizes contribution naturally
