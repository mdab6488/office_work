
### Creating SEO Reports

**Purpose of SEO Reports:**
- **Track progress** toward goals and KPIs
- **Demonstrate value** to stakeholders
- **Identify opportunities** and problems early
- **Guide strategic decisions** with data
- **Maintain accountability** for SEO efforts

**Types of SEO Reports:**

**1. Monthly Performance Reports**
**2. Quarterly Strategic Reports**
**3. Executive Summary Reports**
**4. Technical SEO Audit Reports**
**5. Competitive Analysis Reports**
**6. Campaign-Specific Reports**

---
