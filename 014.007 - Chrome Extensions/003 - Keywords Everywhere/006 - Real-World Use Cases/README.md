### Use Case 5: Affiliate Marketing Niche Selection

**Scenario**: Affiliate marketer choosing between niches.

**Niche Comparison**:

```
Verdict: Main term too competitive, but vegan angle viable
```

**Option 2: "Camping Gear"**
```
Main keyword: 550,000 vol | CPC: $0.95 | Comp: 0.78

Related:
- backpacking gear - 74,000 | Comp: 0.65 (Good)
- ultralight camping gear - 33,100 | Comp: 0.52 (Excellent!)
- camping gear for beginners - 27,100 | Comp: 0.48

Verdict: Multiple low-competition angles available
```

**Option 3: "Standing Desk"**
```
Main keyword: 201,000 vol | CPC: $2.15 | Comp: 0.82

Related:
- best standing desk - 60,500 | Comp: 0.79
- standing desk converter - 49,500 | Comp: 0.68
- electric standing desk - 40,500 | Comp: 0.75
- diy standing desk - 14,800 | Comp: 0.42 (Low!)
```

**Winner: Option 3 - Standing Desk (DIY Angle)**

**Why?**
1. Highest CPC = highest affiliate commissions
2. DIY angle has low competition (0.42)
3. Decent search volume for niche (14,800)
4. Can expand to broader standing desk content later
5. Growing market trend (+23% YoY)

**Content Plan**:
```
1. "DIY Standing Desk: Build for Under $50" (14,800 vol)
```
