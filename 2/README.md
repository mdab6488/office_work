## 2. Technical SEO

Technical SEO is the foundation that allows search engines to crawl, understand, and index your website effectively. Think of it as building a house: you need a solid foundation before adding beautiful interiors (content).

### Why Technical SEO Matters

**Real-Life Impact:**
- **Case Study:** Etsy improved site speed by 0.16 seconds → 12% increase in revenue
- **Case Study:** Pinterest rebuilt their site with better technical SEO → 15% increase in search traffic and sign-ups
- **Stat:** 53% of users abandon sites that take longer than 3 seconds to load
